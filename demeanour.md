- demeanour
  - 发音
    - 英 /dɪ'miːnə/
    - 美 /di'mi:nə/
  - 词义
    - n. 行为
      - Your demeanour is the way you behave, which gives people an impression of your character and feelings
  - 同根词
    - n. demeanor 风度；举止；行为
  - 近义词
    - n. 行为
      - behavior
      - dealing
      - going
      - act
      - work
  - 短语
    - elegant demeanour 风采
  - 例句
    - ...her calm and cheerful demeanour. …她那冷静乐观的态度。
