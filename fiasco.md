- fiasco
  - 发音
    - 英 /fɪ'æskəʊ/
    - 美 /fɪ'æsko/
  - 词义
    - n. 惨败
      - an event that is completely unsuccessful, in a way that is very embarrassing or disappointing
  - 近义词
    - n. 惨败
      - catastrophe
      - smashup
  - 例句
    - The first lecture I ever gave was a complete fiasco . 我第一次讲课糟得一塌糊涂。
  - 补充
