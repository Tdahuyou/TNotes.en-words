- coagulate
  - 发音
    - 英 `/kəu'ægjuleit/`
    - 美 `/ko'æɡjulet/`
  - 词义
    - v. 凝结
    - `if a liquid coagulates, or something coagulates it, it becomes thick and almost solid`
  - 记忆
    - co (一起) + ag (做) + ulate → 做到一起 → 使凝结
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `coagulable` 可凝结的
    - n.
      - `coagulation` 凝固，凝结；凝结物
      - `coagulant` [建][化工] 促凝剂；凝血剂；凝结剂
      - `coagulator` [助剂] 凝结剂；[化工] 凝结器
      - `coagulum` 凝结物；凝固物；凝块
  - 近义词
    - vt. 使…凝结
      - `congeal`
    - vi. 凝结
      - `concrete`
      - `to freeze`
  - 例句
    - `The blood had not coagulated.`
      - 血未凝结。
