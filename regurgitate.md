- regurgitate
  - 发音
    - 英 /rɪ'gɜːdʒɪteɪt/
    - 美 /rɪ'ɡɝdʒɪtet/
  - 词义
    - v. 反刍,（液体）回流
      - to bring food that you have already swallowed, back into your mouth
  - 同根词
    - n. regurgitation 回流；反刍；流回
  - 近义词
    - vi. 回流；回涌；反胃；反刍
      - back flow
      - inverse flow
  - 例句
    - Some birds and animals regurgitate food to feed their young. 有些鸟兽会回吐食物以喂哺幼崽。
  - 补充
