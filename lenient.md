- lenient
  - 发音
    - 英 /'liːnɪənt/
    - 美 /'linɪənt/
  - 词义
    - adj. 宽大的；仁慈的
      - not strict in the way you punish someone or in the standard you expect
    - n. (Lenient)人名；(法)勒尼安
  - 同根词
    - adv. leniently 宽大地；温和地
    - n. leniency 宽大，仁慈；温和
    - n. lenience 仁慈；宽厚
    - n. lenity 慈悲；宽大处理；宽大的行为
  - 近义词
    - adj. 宽大的；仁慈的
      - liberal
      - humane
  - 例句
    - the lenient sentences handed down by some judges 一些法官作出的宽大判决
    - School examiners say that marking has become more lenient in recent years. 学校的阅卷人称近年来评分标准放宽了。
  - 补充
