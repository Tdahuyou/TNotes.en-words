- obstetrics
  - 发音
    - 英 `/əb'stetrɪks/`
    - 美 `/əb'stɛtrɪks/`
  - 词义
    - n. 产科学
    - `the part of medical science that deals with the birth of children`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `obstetrical` 产科的；助产的
    - n.
      - `obstetrician` 产科医师
  - 近义词
    - n. [妇产]产科学，助产术
      - `midwifery`
      - `tocology`
  - 例句
    - `...the American College of Obstetrics and Gynecology.`
      - ...美国产科和妇科学院。
