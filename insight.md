- insight
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'ɪnsaɪt/`
    - 美 `/'ɪn'saɪt/`
  - 词义
    - n. 洞察力；领悟
    - `the ability to understand and realize what people or situations are really like`
  - 记忆
    - in(进入) + sight(眼光) → 眼光深入 → 洞察力
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `insightful` 有深刻见解的，富有洞察力的
  - 近义词
    - n. 洞察力；洞悉
      - `perception`
      - `penetration`
  - 短语
    - `insight into` 深刻理解，洞察… 
    - `gain an insight into` 看透， 识破 
  - 例句
    - `a woman of great insight`
      - 一位极有眼光的妇女
