- restitution
  - 发音
    - 英 /ˌresti'tjuːʃən/
    - 美 /'rɛstə'tʊʃən/
  - 词义
    - n. 归还
      - the act of giving back something that was lost or stolen to its owner, or of paying for damage
  - 记忆
    - re (重新) + stitut (站立) + ion → 重新站过去 → 归还
  - 同根词
    - vt. restitute 复原；归还；补偿
  - 近义词
    - n. 恢复；赔偿；归还
      - recovery
      - satisfaction
      - retrieval
      - renewal
      - return
  - 短语
    - coefficient of restitution 恢复系数；回弹系数
  - 例句
    - The offender must make restitution for the hurt that he or she has caused. 违法者必须为其所造成的伤害作出赔偿。
  - 补充
