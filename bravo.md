- bravo
  - 发音
    - 英 /'brɑːvəu/
    - 美 /,brɑ'vo/
  - 词义
    - n. 亡命徒；喝彩
      - used to show your approval when someone, especially a performer, has done something very well
    - int. 好啊！妙！
      - used to show your approval when someone, especially a performer, has done something very well
  - 记忆
    - “不赖哦” → 喝彩， 拍手叫好
  - 近义词
    - n. 喝彩；亡命徒
      - plaudit
      - acclamation
    - vt. 喝彩
      - to applaud
      - cheer for
    - int. 好极了
      - marvy
  - 例句
    - Bravo! Encore! 好极了！ 再来一个！
  - 补充
