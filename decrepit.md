- decrepit
  - 发音
    - 英 `/di'krepit/`
    - 美 `/dɪ'krɛpɪt/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 衰老的
    - `old and in bad condition`
  - 记忆
    - de + cre (长大) + pit → 衰老的
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `decrepitude` 衰老，老朽
  - 近义词
    - adj. 衰老的；破旧的
      - `shot`
      - `anile`
  - 例句
    - `The buildings were in a decrepit state.`
      - 这些建筑物破旧不堪。
    - `He was a rather decrepit old man.`
      - 他年老体弱。
