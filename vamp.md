- vamp
  - 发音
    - 英 `/væmp/`
    - 美 `/væmp/`
  - 词义
    - n. 鞋面,荡妇
    - `a woman who uses her sexual attractiveness to make men do what she wants`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `vamper` 即席伴奏者；补靴者
  - 近义词
    - vt. 修补；拼凑
      - `patch up`
      - `piece up`
    - n. [服装]鞋面；荡妇
      - `slut`
      - `chippie`
