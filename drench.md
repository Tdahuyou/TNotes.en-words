- drench
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/drentʃ/`
    - 美
      - `/drɛntʃ/`
  - 词义
    - vt.
      - 使湿透
        - `to make something or someone extremely wet`
  - 记忆
    - drench(=drink喝) → 喝水 → 使湿透
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `drenching`
        - 湿透
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 使湿透；给（牲畜）灌药
        - `bedrabble`
    - n.
      - 滂沱大雨；浸液
        - `immersion liquid`
  - 例句
    - `In the early morning they had got drenched in the grass.`
      - 一大清早他们在草丛里弄得浑身湿透。
