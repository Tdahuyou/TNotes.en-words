- frankly
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'fræŋklɪ/`
    - 美 `/'fræŋkli/`
  - 词义
    - adv. 真诚地，坦白地
    - `used to show that you are saying what you really think about something`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `frank` 坦白的，直率的；老实的
    - n.
      - `frank` 免费邮寄特权
      - `frankness` 率直；坦白
    - vt.
      - `frank` 免费邮寄
  - 近义词
    - adv. 真诚地，坦白地
      - `sincerely`
      - `truly`
      - `simply`
      - `honestly`
      - `genuinely`
  - 例句
    - `Frankly, I think the Internet is overrated.`
      - 坦率地说，我认为因特网被高估了。
    - `His behaviour was frankly disgraceful.`
      - 他的行为老实说是可耻的。
