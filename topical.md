- topical
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'tɒpɪk(ə)l/`
    - 美
      - `/'tɑpɪkl/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 有关时事的
        - `Topical is used to describe something that concerns or relates to events that are happening at the present time`
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `topically`
        - 总论地；时事问题地
    - n.
      - `topic`
        - 主题（等于theme）；题目；一般规则；总论
      - `topicality`
        - 时事性；时事性话题
  - 近义词
    - adj.
      - 局部的；论题的；时事问题的；局部地区的
        - `partial`
        - `regional`
        - `local`
  - 短语
    - `topical application`
      - [化]局部施用 
  - 例句
    - `The newscast covers topical events and entertainment.`
      - 该新闻广播涉及时事和娱乐。
