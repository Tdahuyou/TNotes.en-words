- religiously
  - 发音
    - 英 `/undefined/`
    - 美 `/rɪ'lɪdʒəsli/`
  - 词义
    - adv. 虔诚地,笃信地
    - `If you do something religiously, you do it very regularly because you feel you have to`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `religious` 宗教的；虔诚的；严谨的；修道的
    - n.
      - `religious` 修道士；尼姑
      - `religiosity` 虔诚；宗教狂；虚伪信仰
      - `religionism` 宗教狂，笃信宗教
      - `religionist` 笃信宗教者；宗教家
      - `religiousness` 虔敬，笃信
  - 近义词
    - adv. 虔诚地，笃信地
      - `piously`
      - `prayerfully`
  - 例句
    - `Do these exercises religiously every day.`
      - 每天要认真地做这些练习。
