- disport
  - 发音
    - 英 `/dɪ'spɔːrt/`
    - 美 `/dis'pɔ:t/`
  - 词义
    - v. 玩耍，嬉戏
    - `to amuse yourself by doing things that are active and enjoyable – used humorously`
  - 记忆
    - dis(加强) + port(带) → 带走(时间) → 玩耍
  - 近义词
    - vt. 使欢娱；使快乐
      - `joy`
    - vi. 娱乐；玩耍
      - `to entertain`
      - `recreate`
    - n. 娱乐
      - `entertainment`
      - `pleasure`
      - `sport`
      - `recreation`
      - `amusement`
