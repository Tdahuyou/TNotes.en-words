- plunder
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'plʌndə/`
    - 美 `/'plʌndɚ/`
  - 词义
    - v. 抢劫
    - `to steal large amounts of money or property from somewhere, especially while fighting in a war`
  - 记忆
    - pl (看作place， 放) + under (在…下面) → 《加勒比海盗》中海盗们抢劫的宝藏放在孤岛之下 → 抢劫
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `plunderage` 掠夺；盗用船货
  - 近义词
    - n. 抢夺；战利品；掠夺品
      - `prize`
      - `capture`
    - vt. 掠夺；抢劫；侵吞
      - `harry`
      - `despoil`
    - vi. 掠夺；盗窃
      - `prey upon`
      - `pillage`
  - 例句
    - `The rich provinces of Asia Minor were plundered by the invaders.`
      - 小亚细亚的富饶地区遭到侵略者的劫掠。
