- foresight
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'fɔːsaɪt/`
    - 美
      - `/'fɔrsaɪt/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 远见,深谋远虑
        - `the ability to imagine what is likely to happen and to consider this when planning for the future`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `foresighted`
        - 深谋远虑的；有先见之明的；能预料的
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 先见，远见；预见；深谋远虑
        - `prescience`
        - `prevision`
  - 例句
    - `It was an example of the authorities’ lack of foresight .`
      - 这是当权者缺乏远见的一个例证。
