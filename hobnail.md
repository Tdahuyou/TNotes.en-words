- hobnail
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'hɒbneɪl/`
    - 美
      - `/'hɑb,nel/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 鞋钉，平头钉；乡下佬
        - `a short nail with a large head for protecting the soles of heavy footwear `
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `hobnailed`
        - 钉有平头钉的；乡下的
    - v.
      - `hobnailed`
        - 钉平头钉（hobnail的过去分词）
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 鞋钉，[五金]平头钉；乡下佬
        - `yokelry`
        - `jibaro`
