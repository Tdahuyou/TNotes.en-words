- bedeck
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/bi'dek/`
    - 美
      - `/bɪ'dɛk/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 装饰，修饰
        - `to decorate something such as a building or street by hanging things all over it`
  - 记忆
    - be (加强) + deck (装饰) → 装饰
  - 同根词
    - vt.
      - `bedizen`
        - 俗丽地穿着或装饰；过分地打扮
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 装饰；修饰
        - `paint`
        - `hang`
        - `modify`
  - 例句
    - `...flags bedecking the balcony.`
      - ... 装饰阳台的旗子。
