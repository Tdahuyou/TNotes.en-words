- impatience
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ɪm'peɪʃns/`
    - 美 `/ɪm'peʃəns/`
  - 词义
    - n. 急躁；无耐心
    - `annoyance at having to accept delays, other people’s weaknesses etc`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `impatient` 焦躁的；不耐心的
    - adv.
      - `impatiently` 无耐性地
  - 近义词
    - n. 急躁；无耐心
      - `temperament`
      - `precipitance`
  - 例句
    - `A note of impatience had entered his voice.`
      - 他的语气开始透出一丝不耐烦。
