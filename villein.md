- villein
  - 发音
    - 英 /'vɪlən; -eɪn/
    - 美 /'vɪlen/
  - 词义
    - n. [史]农奴,佃农
      - a poor farm worker in the Middle Ages who was given a small piece of land in return for working on the land of a rich lord
  - 同根词
    - n. villeinage 隶农制；隶农身分；隶农土地保有条件，隶农身份
  - 近义词
    - n. 农奴；佃农
      - serf
      - ryot
  - 补充
