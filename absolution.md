- absolution
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ˌæbsə'ljuːʃən/`
    - 美
      - `/,æbsə'luʃən/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 免罪，赦免
        - `when someone is formally forgiven by the Christian Church or a priest for the things they have done wrong`
  - 记忆
    - ab (离开) + solut (松) + ion → 松绑允许离开 → 免罪， 赦免
  - 同根词
    - vt.
      - `absolve`
        - 免除；赦免；宣告…无罪
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - [法]赦免；免罪
        - `acquittal`
        - `indulgency`
  - 例句
    - `Pope Leo gave him absolution.`
      - 教皇列奥赦免了他的罪孽。
