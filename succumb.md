- succumb
  - 发音
    - 英 /sə'kʌm/
    - 美 /sə'kʌm/
  - 词义
    - vi. 屈服，屈从；死亡
      - to stop opposing someone or something that is stronger than you, and allow them to take control
  - 记忆
    - suc(在…下面) + cumb(躺) → 躺在下面， 不再起来 → (因…)死亡
  - 近义词
    - vi. 屈服；死；被压垮
      - to surrender
      - genuflect
  - 例句
    - Don't succumb to the temptation to have just one cigarette. 不要屈服于只抽一支烟的诱惑。
  - 补充
