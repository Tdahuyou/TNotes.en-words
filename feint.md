- feint
  - 发音
    - 英 /feint/
    - 美 /fent/
  - 词义
    - n. 伪装，假装，佯攻
      - a mock attack or movement designed to distract an adversary, as in a military manoeuvre or in boxing, fencing, etc 
  - 记忆
    - 与feign一起记
  - 近义词
    - n. 佯攻；伪装
      - dissemblance
      - stalking-horse
    - vi. 佯攻；假装
      - act
      - put on
    - vt. 佯攻
      - juke
    - adj. 假的
      - spurious
      - unreal
