- punctuate
  - 发音
    - 英 /'pʌŋktjueit/
    - 美 /ˈpʌŋktʃuet/
  - 词义
    - v. 加标点于；不时打断；强调
      - to divide written work into sentences, phrases etc using  comma s  ,  full stop s  etc
  - 记忆
    - punct (刺) + uate → 锥刺股来提醒自己学习的重要性 → 强调
  - 同根词
    - n. punctuation 标点；标点符号
  - 近义词
    - vt. 不时打断；强调；加标点于
      - emphasize
      - stress
      - highlight
  - 例句
    - The silence was occasionally punctuated by laughter. 寂静间或被笑声打破。
