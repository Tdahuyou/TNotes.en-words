- transfusion
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/træns'fjuːʒ(ə)n; trɑːns-; -nz-/`
    - 美
      - `/trænz'fjʊʒən/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 输血
        - `the process of putting blood from one person’s body into the body of someone else as a medical treatment`
  - 同根词
    - vt.
      - `transfuse`
        - 输血；灌输；注入
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - [临床]输血；输液；倾注；灌输
        - `blood filtering`
        - `implantation`
  - 短语
    - `blood transfusion`
      - 输血 
  - 例句
    - `A blood transfusion saved his life.`
      - 输血挽救了他的生命。
