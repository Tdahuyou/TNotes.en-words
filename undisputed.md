- undisputed
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ʌndɪ'spjuːtɪd/`
    - 美 `/,ʌndɪ'spjutɪd/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 无可置辩的
    - `known to be definitely true`
  - 近义词
    - adj. 无可争辩的；无异议的；毫无疑问的
      - `unarguable`
      - `incontestable`
  - 例句
    - `Doctors found undisputed evidence of nerve damage.`
      - 医生发现了神经受损的确凿证据。
