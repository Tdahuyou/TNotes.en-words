- tutor
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'tuːtər/`
    - 美 `/'tʊtɚ/`
  - 词义
    - v. 辅导
    - `to teach someone as a tutor`
    - n. 导师；家庭教师
    - `someone who gives private lessons to one student or a small group, and is paid directly by them`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `tutorial` 辅导的；家庭教师的，个别指导的
    - n.
      - `tutorial` 个别指导
      - `tutelage` 监护；指导
      - `tutee` 受指导者；受监护者
      - `tutorship` 辅导；教师职位
  - 近义词
    - vt. 辅导；约束
      - `restrict`
      - `hold oneself in`
    - n. 导师；家庭教师；助教
      - `mentors`
      - `teacher counselor`
  - 短语
    - `private tutor` 私人教师，家庭教师 
    - `tutor system` 导师制 
  - 例句
    - `He was privately tutored.`
      - 他是由家庭教师教的。
