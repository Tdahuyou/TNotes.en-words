- venerate
  - 发音
    - 英 /'venəˌreit/
    - 美 /'vɛnəret/
  - 词义
    - v. 崇敬
      - to honour or respect someone or something because they are old, holy, or connected with the past
  - 记忆
    - vener (尊敬) + ate → 崇敬
  - 同根词
    - adj. venerable 庄严的，值得尊敬的；珍贵的
    - n. veneration 尊敬；崇拜
    - n. venerability 尊敬；庄严；历史悠久
    - n. venerator 崇拜者；尊敬者
  - 近义词
    - vt. 崇敬，尊敬
      - honor
      - regard
      - worship
  - 例句
    - a symbol of Arab courage, to be venerated for generations 受到世代尊崇的阿拉伯人勇气的象征
