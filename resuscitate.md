- resuscitate
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ri'sʌsiteit/`
    - 美
      - `/rɪ'sʌsɪtet/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 复苏；复活
        - `to make someone breathe again or become conscious after they have almost died`
  - 记忆
    - re + sus (在下面) + cit (引起) + ate → 枯木逢春， 从土下起 → 复苏
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `resuscitation`
        - 复苏；复兴；复活
      - `resuscitator`
        - 使复活的人；人工呼吸器
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 使复苏；使复兴
        - `reinvigorate`
    - vi.
      - 恢复；复兴
        - `restore`
        - `recover from`
  - 例句
    - `Doctors managed to resuscitate him.`
      - 医生把他抢救了过来。
