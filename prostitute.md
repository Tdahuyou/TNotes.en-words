- prostitute
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'prɒstɪtjuːt/`
    - 美 `/'prɑstətut/`
  - 词义
    - v. 使沦为妓女
    - adj. 卖淫的；堕落的
    - n. 妓女
    - `someone, especially a woman, who earns money by having sex with people`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `prostitution` 卖淫；滥用；出卖灵魂
  - 近义词
    - adj. 卖淫的；堕落的
      - `fallen`
      - `vicious`
    - n. 妓女
      - `sporting woman`
      - `hetaerae`
      - `poule`
      - `sporting lady`
  - 短语
    - `prostitute oneself` 卖淫 
  - 例句
    - `He admitted last week he paid for sex with a prostitute.`
      - 他承认上星期曾和一名妓女有过性交易。
