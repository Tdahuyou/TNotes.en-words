- underestimate
  - 发音
    - 英 /ʌndər'estɪmeɪt/
    - 美 //ˌʌndərˈestɪmeɪt//
  - 词义
    - vt& n. 估计不足，低估
      - to think or guess that something is smaller, cheaper, easier etc than it really is
  - 同根词
    - n. underestimation 低估；过低之估价
  - 近义词
    - vt. 低估；看轻
      - disappreciate
      - sell short
    - n. 低估
      - disappreciation
  - 例句
    - Fourteen percent may be an underestimate. 14%可能是低估的。
  - 补充
