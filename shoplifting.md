- shoplifting
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'ʃɒplɪftɪŋ/`
    - 美 `/'ʃɑp'lɪftɪŋ/`
  - 词义
    - n. 在商店盗窃
    - `the crime of stealing things from shops, for example by hiding them in a bag or under your clothes`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `shop` 商店；店铺
      - `shopper` 购物者；顾客
      - `shoplifter` 商店扒手
    - vi.
      - `shop` 购物；买东西
      - `shoplift` 从商店中偷商品
    - vt.
      - `shop` 购物
      - `shoplift` 从商店中偷商品
  - 例句
    - `She had been falsely accused of shoplifting in a clothing store.`
      - 她被冤枉在一家服装店里行窃。
