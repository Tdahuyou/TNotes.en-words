- duplicity
  - 发音
    - 英 /djuː'plisiti/
    - 美 /du'plɪsəti/
  - 词义
    - n. 奸诈，狡猾
      - dishonest behaviour that is intended to deceive someone
  - 记忆
    - du (双) + pli (重) + city → 有双重态度 → 狡猾
  - 近义词
    - n. 口是心非；表里不一；不诚实
      - dishonesty
      - perfidy
  - 例句
    - Malcolm believed he was guilty of duplicity in his private dealings. 马尔科姆相信他在为其私底下行为的奸诈内疚着。
  - 补充
