- foreword
  - 发音
    - 英 /'fɔːwɜːd/
    - 美 /'fɔrwɝd/
  - 词义
    - n. 序；前言
      - a short piece of writing at the beginning of a book that introduces the book or its writer
  - 近义词
    - n. 序；前言
      - prolegomena
      - perface
  - 例句
    - She has written the foreword to a book of recipes. 她为一本食谱写了前言。
  - 补充
