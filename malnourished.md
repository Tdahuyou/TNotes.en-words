- malnourished
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ˌmælˈnʌrɪʃt/`
    - 美
      - `/,mæl'nɝɪʃt/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 营养不良的
        - `someone who is malnourished is ill or weak because they have not had enough good food to eat`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `malnutrition`
        - 营养失调，营养不良
  - 近义词
    - adj.
      - 营养不良的；营养失调的
        - `ill-fed`
        - `dystrophic`
  - 例句
    - `malnourished children`
      - 营养不良的儿童
