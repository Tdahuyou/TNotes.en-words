- clumsy
  - 发音
    - 英 /'klʌmzi/
    - 美 /'klʌmzi/
  - 词义
    - adj. 笨拙的；不得体的，冒犯人的；复杂难懂的，难处理的
      - moving or doing things in a careless way, especially so that you drop things, knock into things etc
  - 同根词
    - adv. clumsily 笨拙地；粗陋地
    - n. clumsiness 笨拙；粗陋；不漂亮
  - 近义词
    - adj. 笨拙的
      - awkward
      - inept
  - 例句
    - A clumsy waiter spilled wine all over her new skirt. 一名笨手笨脚的服务员把葡萄酒洒得她新裙子上到处都是。
    - a clumsy attempt to catch the ball 笨拙的接球动作
