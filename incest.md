- incest
  - 发音
    - 英 /'ɪnsest/
    - 美 /'ɪnsɛst/
  - 词义
    - n. 乱伦；近亲通婚
      - sex between people who are closely related in a family
  - 同根词
    - adj. incestuous 乱伦的；血亲相奸的；犯乱伦罪的
  - 近义词
    - n. 乱伦；近亲通婚
      - fornication
  - 例句
    - Abortions would only be allowed in cases of rape or incest. 只有被强奸或乱伦受孕才允许堕胎。
