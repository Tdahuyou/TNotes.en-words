- flog
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/flɒg/`
    - 美
      - `/flɑɡ/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 鞭打，鞭策；迫使
        - `to beat a person or animal with a whip or stick`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `flogging`
        - 鞭打
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 鞭打，鞭策；迫使
        - `hide`
        - `pressure`
    - vi.
      - 剧烈抖动；除尘
        - `dust removal`
        - `dust extraction`
  - 例句
    - `He was publicly flogged and humiliated.`
      - 他当众受到鞭打和羞辱。
