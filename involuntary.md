- involuntary
  - 发音
    - 英 /ɪn'vɒlənt(ə)rɪ/
    - 美 /ɪn'vɑləntɛri/
  - 词义
    - adj. 无意识的；自然而然的；不知不觉的
      - an involuntary movement, sound, reaction etc is one that you make suddenly and without intending to because you cannot control yourself
  - 同根词
    - adv. involuntarily 无心地；不自觉地；偶然地
  - 近义词
    - adj. 无意识的；自然而然的；不知不觉的
      - mechanical
      - automatic
      - unconscious
      - spontaneous
  - 例句
    - When Willie tapped on the window, Miguel gave an involuntary jump. 威利敲了敲窗户，米格尔不由自主地跳了起来。
  - 补充
