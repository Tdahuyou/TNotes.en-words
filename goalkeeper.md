- goalkeeper
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'gəʊlkiːpə/`
    - 美
      - `/'ɡolkipɚ/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 守门员
        - `the player in a sports team whose job is to try to stop the ball going into the goal`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `goalie`
        - 守门员（等于goalkeeper）
      - `goaltender`
        - （足球等运动的）守门员（等于goalkeeper）
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 守门员
        - `netkeeper`
        - `wicket-keeper`
  - 例句
    - `...the league's two best goalkeepers.`
      - ...这届联赛的两名最佳守门员。
