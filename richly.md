- richly
  - 发音
    - 英 /'rɪtʃlɪ/
    - 美 /'rɪtʃli/
  - 词义
    - adv. 富裕地,丰富地
      - If you are richly rewarded for doing something, you get something very valuable or pleasant in return for doing it
  - 同根词
    - adj. rich 富有的；肥沃的；昂贵的
    - n. richness 丰富；富裕；肥沃；华美
  - 近义词
    - adv. 丰富地；富裕地；完全地
      - totally
      - entirely
      - completely
      - absolutely
      - thoroughly
      - strictly
      - fully
  - 例句
    - It is a difficult book to read, but it richly rewards the effort. 这是本难读的书，不过它会丰厚回报为之所付出的努力。
  - 补充
