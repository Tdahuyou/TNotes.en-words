- treasurer
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'treʒ(ə)rə/`
    - 美
      - `/'trɛʒərɚ/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 司库,财务主管
        - `someone who is officially responsible for the money for an organization, club, political party etc`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `treasure`
        - 财富，财产；财宝；珍品
      - `treasury`
        - 国库，金库；财政部；宝库
      - `treasurership`
        - 会计员之职
    - vt.
      - `treasure`
        - 珍爱；珍藏
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 会计；司库；财务主管；出纳员
        - `teller`
        - `financial executive`
