- captivate
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'kæptɪveɪt/`
    - 美
      - `/'kæptɪvet/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 迷住,迷惑
        - `to attract someone very much, and hold their attention`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `captive`
        - 被俘虏的；被迷住的
      - `captivating`
        - 迷人的；有魅力的
    - n.
      - `captive`
        - 俘虏；迷恋者
      - `captivation`
        - 魅力；着迷；迷惑
    - v.
      - `captivating`
        - 使…着迷（captivate的ing形式）
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 迷住，迷惑
        - `possess`
        - `spell`
  - 例句
    - `I was captivated by her brilliant mind.`
      - 我为她的聪慧所迷。
