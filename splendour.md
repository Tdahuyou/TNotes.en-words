- splendour
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'splendə/`
    - 美 `/ˈsplɛndɚ/`
  - 词义
    - n. 壮观，杰出，壮丽
    - `impressive beauty, especially of a large building or large place`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `splendor` 光彩；壮丽；显赫
  - 近义词
    - n. 显赫（等于splendor）；光彩壮丽
      - `eminence`
  - 例句
    - `The palace has now been restored to its original splendour.`
      - 宫殿如今已恢复了它昔日的辉煌。
