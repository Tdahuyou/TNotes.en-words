- came
  - 发音
    - 英 /keɪm/
    - 美 /keɪm/
  - 词义
    - v. 来（come 的过去式）
      - Moved towards the speaker or a particular place or position.
  - 同根词
    - v. come 来
    - n. coming 到来；来临
    - adj. upcoming 即将到来的
  - 近义词
    - v. arrived 到达；抵达
      - Reached a destination.
  - 短语
    - come back 回来
    - come from 来自
    - come on 快点；加油
  - 例句
    - She came to see me yesterday. 她昨天来看我。
    - I came because you called me. 因为你打电话给我，所以我来了。
    - They came here last week. 他们上个星期来到这里。
