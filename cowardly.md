- cowardly
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'kaʊədlɪ/`
    - 美 `/'kauədli/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 怯懦的，懦弱的；胆小的
    - `If you describe someone as cowardly, you disapprove of them because they are easily frightened and avoid doing dangerous and difficult things`
    - adv. 胆怯地
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `coward` 胆小的，懦怯的
    - n.
      - `coward` 懦夫，懦弱的人
      - `cowardice` 怯懦；胆小
      - `cowardliness` 胆怯；懦弱
  - 近义词
    - adj. 怯懦的，懦弱的；胆小的
      - `faint-hearted`
      - `unmanly`
    - adv. 胆怯地
      - `cravenly`
      - `pusillanimously`
  - 例句
    - `I was too cowardly to complain.`
      - 我胆小得都不敢抱怨。
