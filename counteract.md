- counteract
  - 发音
    - 英 /ˌkauntər'ækt/
    - 美 /,kaʊntɚ'ækt/
  - 词义
    - vt. 消除；抵消
      - to reduce or prevent the bad effect of something, by doing something that has the opposite effect
  - 记忆
    - counter(反) + act(动作) → 做相反的动作 → 消除； 抵消
  - 同根词
    - adj. counteractive 反作用的；抵抗的；反对的
    - n. counterbalance 平衡力；自动抵销
    - n. counteraction 中和；反对的行动；抵抗；反动
    - n. counteractive 反作用；抵抗，对抗；反对
    - vt. counterbalance 使平衡；抵消
  - 近义词
    - vt. 抵消；中和；阻碍
      - offset
      - kill
      - slow
  - 例句
    - They gave him drugs to counteract his withdrawal symptoms. 他们给他服药以消除他的脱瘾症状。
