- debar
  - 发音
    - 英 /dɪ'bɑːr/
    - 美 /dɪ'bɑr/
  - 词义
    - v. 阻止
      - to officially prevent someone from doing something
  - 记忆
    - de(加强) + bar(阻拦)  → 阻止
  - 同根词
    - n. debarment 排除，阻止
  - 近义词
    - vt. 阻止；排除；防止
      - eliminate
      - stem
      - block
      - dispute
      - prohibit
  - 例句
    - ...the decision to debar particular immigrants. ... 禁止特殊移民的决定。
  - 补充
