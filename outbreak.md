- outbreak
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'autbreɪk/`
    - 美
      - `/'aʊtbrek/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 发作；爆发
        - `if there is an outbreak of fighting or disease in an area, it suddenly starts to happen`
  - 记忆
    - 来自词组break out(突发， 爆发)
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - （战争的）爆发；（疾病的）发作
        - `explosion`
        - `burst`
        - `fit`
    - vi.
      - 爆发
        - `burst`
        - `blow up`
  - 例句
    - `a cholera outbreak`
      - 霍乱的爆发
