- surveyor
  - 发音
    - 英 `/sə'veɪə/`
    - 美 `/sɚ'veɚ/`
  - 词义
    - n. 测量员,检查员
    - `someone whose job is to examine the condition of a building, or to measure and record the details of an area of land`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `survey` 调查；测量；审视；纵览
      - `surveying` （土地）测量；考察
    - vi.
      - `survey` 测量土地
    - vt.
      - `survey` 调查；勘测；俯瞰
  - 近义词
    - n. [测]测量员；检验员
      - `examiner`
      - `hecker`
  - 例句
    - `...the surveyor's maps of the Queen Alexandra Range.`
      - …亚历山德拉皇后山脉勘测图。
