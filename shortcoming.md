- shortcoming
  - 发音
    - 英 /'ʃɔːtkʌmɪŋ/
    - 美 /'ʃɔrtkʌmɪŋ/
  - 词义
    - n. 缺点,短处
      - a fault or weakness that makes someone or something less successful or effective than they should be
  - 记忆
    - short(短)+coming→短处， 缺点
  - 近义词
    - n. 缺点；短处
      - defect
      - deficiency
      - disadvantage
      - vice
      - fault
  - 例句
    - Peter was painfully aware of his own shortcomings. 彼得痛苦地认识到了自己的短处。
  - 补充
