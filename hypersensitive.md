- hypersensitive
  - 发音
    - 英 /haɪpə'sensɪtɪv/
    - 美 /,haɪpɚ'sɛnsətɪv/
  - 词义
    - adj. 过份敏感的
      - if someone is hypersensitive to a drug, substance etc, their body reacts very badly to it
  - 同根词
    - n. hypersensitivity 过敏症；高灵敏度
  - 近义词
    - adj. [医]过敏的；高灵敏度的；超感光度的
      - irritable
      - touchy
  - 例句
    - Student teachers were hypersensitive to any criticism of their performance. 实习教师对任何针对他们表现的批评意见都异常敏感。
  - 补充
