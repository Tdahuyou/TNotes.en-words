- persecute
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'pɜːsɪkjuːt/`
    - 美 `/'pɝsɪkjut/`
  - 词义
    - v. 迫害；困扰；同…捣乱
    - `to treat someone cruelly or unfairly over a period of time, especially because of their religious or political beliefs`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `persecution` 迫害；烦扰
      - `persecutor` 虐待者；迫害者
  - 近义词
    - vt. 迫害；困扰；同…捣乱
      - `beset`
      - `eat at`
  - 例句
    - `The Puritans left England to escape being persecuted.`
      - 清教徒离开了英国以躲避迫害。
