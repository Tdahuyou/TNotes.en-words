- fumigate
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'fjuːmɪgeɪt/`
    - 美
      - `/'fjumɪɡet/`
  - 词义
    - vt.
      - 烟熏，香薰
        - `to remove disease,  bacteria  , insects etc from somewhere using chemicals, smoke, or gas`
  - 记忆
    - fum(烟) + ig(开动) + ate → 烟熏
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `fumigation`
        - 烟熏法；熏烟消毒法
      - `fumigant`
        - 熏剂；熏蒸消毒剂
      - `fumigator`
        - 熏蒸消毒器；烟熏器
