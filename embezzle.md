- embezzle
  - 发音
    - 英 `/im'bezl/`
    - 美 `/ɪm'bɛzl/`
  - 词义
    - v. 盗用；挪用
    - `to steal money from the place where you work`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `embezzlement` 侵占；挪用；盗用
      - `embezzler` 侵占公款犯，盗用公款者
  - 近义词
    - vt. 盗用；挪用；贪污
      - `misappropriate`
  - 例句
    - `Two managers were charged with embezzling $400,000.`
      - 两名经理被控挪用了40万美元。
