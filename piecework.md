- piecework
  - 发音
    - 英 /'piːswɜːk/
    - 美 /'piswɝk/
  - 词义
    - n. 计件工作
      - work for which you are paid according to the number of things you produce rather than the number of hours that you spend working
  - 近义词
    - n. [经管]计件工作
      - taskwork
  - 例句
    - bargaining over piecework rates 就计件工作报酬进行商讨
  - 补充
