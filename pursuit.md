- pursuit
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/pər'suːt/`
    - 美
      - `/pɚ'sut/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 追求；
        - `when someone tries to get, achieve, or find something in a determined way`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `pursuant`
        - 依据的；追赶的；随后的
    - adv.
      - `pursuant`
        - 根据；依照
    - n.
      - `pursuing`
        - 追求；追逐；追赶
      - `pursuance`
        - 追求；从事；追踪；实行
      - `pursuer`
        - 追求者；追捕者；研究者；原告
      - `pursual`
        - 追赶；追踪
    - v.
      - `pursuing`
        - 从事；追赶（pursue的ing形式）
    - vi.
      - `pursue`
        - 追赶；继续进行
    - vt.
      - `pursue`
        - 继续；从事；追赶；纠缠
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 追赶，追求；职业，工作
        - `profession`
        - `employment`
        - `career`
        - `calling`
        - `job`
  - 短语
    - `in pursuit of`
      - 寻求，追求 
    - `in hot pursuit`
      - 穷追不舍；紧随其后 
  - 例句
    - `The quarterback sprinted toward the end zone with Jansen in hot pursuit (= following closely behind ) .`
      - 那名四分卫快速冲向底线区，詹森穷追不舍。
