- hijack
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'haɪdʒæk/`
    - 美 `/'haɪdʒæk/`
  - 词义
    - v. 抢劫；揩油
    - `to use violence or threats to take control of a plane, vehicle, or ship`
    - n. 劫持；威逼；敲诈
    - `when a plane, vehicle etc is hijacked`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `hijacker` 强盗；抢劫者
  - 近义词
    - vt. 抢劫；揩油
      - `rob of`
      - `spoliate`
    - n. 劫持；威逼；敲诈
      - `chantage`
      - `extortion`
  - 例句
    - `The airliner was hijacked by a group of terrorists.`
      - 该航班被一帮恐怖分子劫持。
