- parquet
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'pɑːkɪ; 'pɑːkeɪ/`
    - 美 `/pɑr'ke/`
  - 词义
    - n. 镶木地板,正厅
    - `small flat blocks of wood fitted together in a pattern that cover the floor of a room`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `parquetry` [建] 镶木地板；拼花地板
  - 短语
    - `parquet floor` 镶花地板；镶木地板 
  - 例句
    - `a parquet floor`
      - 镶木地板
