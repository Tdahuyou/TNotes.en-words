- prefigure
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ˌpriː'fɪgjər/`
    - 美
      - `/pri'fɪɡjʊr/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 预示；预想
        - `to be a sign that something will happen later`
  - 记忆
    - pre(提前) + figure(形象) → 提前想好形象 → 预想
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `prefigurative`
        - 预示的；青年价值取向社会的
    - n.
      - `prefiguration`
        - 预示；预想；征兆
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 预示；预想
        - `indicate`
        - `forecast`
        - `shadow`
        - `announce`
  - 例句
    - `The wall through Berlin was finally ruptured, prefiguring the reunification of Germany.`
      - 柏林墙终于倒塌了，预示着德国的重新统一。
