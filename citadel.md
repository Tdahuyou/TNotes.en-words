- citadel
  - 发音
    - 英 /'sɪtədəl/
    - 美 /ˈsɪtədəl/
  - 词义
    - n. 堡垒，要塞
      - a strong  fort (= small castle )  built in the past as a place where people could go for safety if their city was attacked
  - 近义词
    - n. 城堡；大本营；避难处
      - castle
      - alcazar
  - 例句
    - The citadel at Besançon towered above the river. 贝桑松城堡高过河面。
