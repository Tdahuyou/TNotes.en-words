- bronchitis
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/brɔŋ'kaitis/`
    - 美
      - `/brɑŋ'kaɪtɪs/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 【医】支气管炎
        - `an illness that affects your bronchial tubes and makes you cough`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `bronchial`
        - 支气管的
      - `bronchitic`
        - 支气管炎的
      - `bronchoscopic`
        - 支气管镜的
    - n.
      - `bronchodilator`
        - [内科][药] 支气管扩张剂；支气管扩张器
  - 短语
    - `chronic bronchitis`
      - 慢性支气管炎 
    - `acute bronchitis`
      - 急性支气管炎 
  - 例句
    - `He was in bed with bronchitis.`
      - 他身患支气管炎，卧病在床。
