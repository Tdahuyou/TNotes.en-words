- bequest
  - 发音
    - 英 `/bɪ'kwest/`
    - 美 `/bi'kwest/`
  - 词义
    - n. 遗产,遗赠
    - `money or property that you arrange to give to someone after your death`
  - 同根词
    - vt.
      - `bequeath` 遗赠；把…遗赠给；把…传下去
  - 近义词
    - n. [法]遗产；遗赠
      - `heritage`
      - `inheritance`
      - `legacy`
  - 例句
    - `a bequest of $5,000`
      - 五千美元的遗产
