- acquiescence
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ˌækwi'esəns/`
    - 美
      - `/'ækwɪ'ɛsns/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 默许
        - `Acquiescence is agreement to do what someone wants, or acceptance of what they do even though you do not agree with it`
  - 记忆
    - ac (向…) + qui (安静的) + escence → 向某种行为表示安静 → 默许
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `acquiescent`
        - 默认的；默许的；甘心接受的
    - vi.
      - `acquiesce`
        - 默许；勉强同意
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - [法]默许；默从
        - `tacit consent`
        - `condonement`
  - 例句
    - `Caitlin smiled her acquiescence.`
      - 凯特琳以微笑默许。
