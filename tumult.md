- tumult
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'tjuːmʌlt/`
    - 美
      - `/'tumʌlt/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 骚动；烦乱
        - `a confused, noisy, and excited situation, often caused by a large crowd`
  - 记忆
    - tumu (看作tumour， 癌症) + lt → 谈癌色变， 让人烦乱 → 烦乱
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `tumultuous`
        - 吵闹的；骚乱的；狂暴的
    - adv.
      - `tumultuously`
        - 喧闹地；骚乱地
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 骚动；骚乱；吵闹；激动
        - `combustion`
        - `row`
        - `disturbance`
        - `disorder`
        - `sound`
  - 例句
    - `I could simply not be heard in the tumult.`
      - 一片嘈杂声中别人根本听不到我说话。
