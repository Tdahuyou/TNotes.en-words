- ignominy
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'ɪgnəmɪnɪ/`
    - 美 `/'ɪɡnəmɪni/`
  - 词义
    - n. 耻辱；不体面；丑行
    - `an event or situation that makes you feel ashamed or embarrassed, especially in public`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `ignominious` 可耻的；下流的
    - adv.
      - `ignominiously` 可耻地
  - 近义词
    - n. 耻辱；不体面；丑行
      - `opprobrium`
      - `stigma`
  - 例句
    - `...the ignominy of being fired.`
      - ...被开除的羞辱。
