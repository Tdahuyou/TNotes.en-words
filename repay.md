- repay
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/riː'peɪ; rɪ-/`
    - 美
      - `/ri'pɛi/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 偿还,报答
        - `to pay back money that you have borrowed`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `repayable`
        - 可偿还的；可报复的
    - n.
      - `repayment`
        - 偿还；[金融] 付还
  - 近义词
    - vi.
      - 偿还；报答；报复
        - `return`
        - `make the payments`
    - vt.
      - 回报；报复；付还
        - `get back on`
        - `get even with`
  - 例句
    - `I’d like to buy them something to repay all their kindness.`
      - 我想给他们买点东西来报答他们的好意。
