- pageant
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'pædʒənt/`
    - 美
      - `/'pædʒənt/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 盛会，庆典，游行
        - `A pageant is a colourful public procession, show, or ceremony. Pageants are usually held outdoors and often celebrate events or people from history. `
  - 记忆
    - page (页) + ant (蚂蚁) → 一页蚂蚁浩浩荡荡游行 → 盛会， 庆典， 游行
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `pageantry`
        - 壮观，华丽；露天历史剧
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 盛会；游行；虚饰；露天表演
        - `parade`
        - `frills`
  - 例句
    - `...a historical pageant of kings and queens.`
      - …一场历史上的国王和王后们的盛大庆典。
