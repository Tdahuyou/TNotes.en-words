- peccadillo
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ˌpekə'diləu/`
    - 美 `/,pekə'diləu/`
  - 词义
    - n. 轻罪，小过失
    - `something bad which someone does, especially involving sex, which is not regarded as very serious or important`
  - 记忆
    - pecca (过失， 罪行) + dillo (小) → 小过失
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `peccable` 易犯过失的；易犯罪的
      - `peccant` 犯罪的；有过失的
  - 近义词
    - n. 轻罪；小过失；小瑕疵
      - `misdemeanor`
      - `venial sin`
  - 例句
    - `The public is willing to forgive him for his peccadillos.`
      - 公众愿意原谅他的小过失。
