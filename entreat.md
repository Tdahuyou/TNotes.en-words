- entreat
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ɪn'triːt/`
    - 美 `/ɪn'trit/`
  - 词义
    - v. 乞求，恳求，请求
    - `to ask someone, in a very emotional way, to do something for you`
  - 记忆
    - en(在…里面) + treat(拉) → 在里面拉 → 乞求， 恳求
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `entreatingly` 乞求地；哀求地
    - n.
      - `entreaty` 恳求；乞求
  - 近义词
    - vt. 恳求；请求
      - `pray for`
      - `appeal for`
    - vi. 恳求；乞求
      - `pray for`
      - `cry on`
  - 例句
    - `He entreated them to delay their departure.`
      - 他恳求他们晚些再走。
