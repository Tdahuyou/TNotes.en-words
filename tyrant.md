- tyrant
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'taɪr(ə)nt/`
    - 美
      - `/'taɪrənt/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 暴君
        - `a ruler who has complete power and uses it in a cruel and unfair way`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `tyrannical`
        - 残暴的；暴君的；专横的
    - n.
      - `tyrannicide`
        - 诛戮暴君者；诛弑暴君
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 暴君
        - `Pharaoh`
        - `Caesar`
  - 例句
    - `The country had long been ruled by tyrants.`
      - 这个国家长期受暴君统治。
