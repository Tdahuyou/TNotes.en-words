- trepan
  - 发音
    - 英 `/trɪ'pæn/`
    - 美 `/trɪ'pæn/`
  - 词义
    - n. 环锯,钻孔机,陷阱
    - `an instrument resembling a carpenter's brace and bit formerly used to remove circular sections of bone (esp from the skull) `
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `trephination` [外科] 环钻术；环锯术
  - 近义词
    - n. 环锯；钻孔机
      - `drilling machine`
      - `driller`
    - vt. 欺骗；诱入圈套；用环锯行手术
      - `sell`
      - `fox`
      - `kid`
