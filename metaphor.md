- metaphor
  - 发音
    - 英 /'metəfər/
    - 美 /'mɛtəfɚ/
  - 词义
    - n. 隐喻，暗喻
      - a way of describing something by referring to it as something different and suggesting that it has similar qualities to that thing
  - 记忆
    - meta(变化) + phor(带来) → 给意义带来变化 → 隐喻， 暗喻
  - 同根词
    - adj. metaphorical 比喻性的，隐喻性的
    - adv. metaphorically 隐喻地；用比喻
  - 近义词
    - n. 暗喻，隐喻；比喻说法
      - parabole
  - 例句
    - She uses some wonderful images and metaphors in her writing. 她在作品中用了一些绝妙的比喻和暗喻。
    - a very creative use of metaphor 隐喻的独到运用
