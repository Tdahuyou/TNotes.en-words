- unrelenting
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ʌnrɪ'lentɪŋ/`
    - 美 `/,ʌnrɪ'lɛntɪŋ/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 无情的；不屈不挠的；不松懈的
    - `an unpleasant situation that is unrelenting continues for a long time without stopping`
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `unrelentingly` 无情地
  - 近义词
    - adj. 无情的；不屈不挠的；不松懈的
      - `tough`
      - `relentless`
      - `inexorable`
      - `ruthless`
  - 例句
    - `the unrelenting pressures of the job`
      - 持续的工作压力
