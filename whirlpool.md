- whirlpool
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'wɜːrlpuːl/`
    - 美
      - `/'wɝlpul/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 旋涡
        - `a powerful current of water that spins around and can pull things down into it`
  - 记忆
    - whirl(旋转， 回旋) + pool(水池) → 旋涡
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `whirling`
        - 旋转的；涡流的
    - n.
      - `whirling`
        - 旋转；涡流；回转
    - v.
      - `whirling`
        - 旋转；眩晕；卷走；疾走（whirl的ing形式）
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 漩涡，涡流；混乱，纷乱
        - `chaos`
        - `disorder`
        - `confusion`
        - `involvement`
        - `mix`
  - 短语
    - `whirlpool bath`
      - 漩涡浴 
  - 例句
    - `people who are caught in a whirlpool of grief`
      - 沉浸在极度悲伤中的人们
