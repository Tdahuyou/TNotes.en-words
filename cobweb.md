- cobweb
  - 发音
    - 英 /'kɒbweb/
    - 美 /'kɑb'wɛb/
  - 词义
    - n. 蜘蛛网；蛛丝；圈套
      - a net of sticky threads made by a  spider  to catch insects, that is inside a building and has not been removed
    - vt. 使布满蛛网；使混乱
  - 同根词
    - adj. cobwebby 蛛网密布的；蛛网似的；布满蜘蛛网的
  - 近义词
    - n. 蜘蛛网；蛛丝；圈套
      - mesh
      - trap
    - vt. 使布满蛛网；使混乱
      - cloud
      - confuse
  - 例句
    - The windows are cracked and covered in cobwebs. 窗户裂开了，上面布满蜘蛛网。
  - 补充
