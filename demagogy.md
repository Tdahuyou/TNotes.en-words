- demagogy
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'deməɡɒɡɪ/`
    - 美 `/'dɛmə,godʒi/`
  - 词义
    - n. 煽动家的方法与行为
    - `You can refer to a method of political rule as demagogy or demagoguery if you disapprove of it because you think it involves appealing to people's emotions rather than using reasonable arguments`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `demagogic` 蛊惑人心的；煽动性的
      - `demagogical` 煽动的
    - n.
      - `demagogue` 煽动者；煽动家；煽动政治家
      - `demagoguery` 散布谣言；煽动行为；群众煽动
      - `demagog` 群众领导者；煽动家
