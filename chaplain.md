- chaplain
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'tʃæplɪn/`
    - 美 `/'tʃæplɪn/`
  - 词义
    - n. 牧师；专职教士
    - `a priest or other religious minister responsible for the religious needs of a club, the army, a hospital etc`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `chaplaincy` 礼拜堂牧师之职位或任期
  - 近义词
    - n. 牧师；专职教士
      - `minister`
      - `priest`
      - `preacher`
  - 例句
    - `the prison chaplain`
      - 监狱牧师
