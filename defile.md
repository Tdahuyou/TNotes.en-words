- defile
  - 发音
    - 英 `/dɪ'faɪl/`
    - 美 `/dɪ'faɪl/`
  - 词义
    - v. 污损，弄脏；染污
    - `to make something less pure and good, especially by showing no respect`
    - n. 狭谷；隘路
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `defilement` 污秽；弄脏；污辱
      - `defiler` 亵渎者；玷污者；蝎子
  - 近义词
    - vt. 污损，弄脏；染污
      - `soil`
      - `mud`
      - `spot`
      - `dirty`
    - n. 狭谷；隘路
      - `narrow`
      - `cajon`
  - 例句
    - `Hallam’s tomb had been defiled and looted.`
      - 哈勒姆的坟墓遭到了亵渎和洗劫。
