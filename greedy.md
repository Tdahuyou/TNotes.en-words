- greedy
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'griːdɪ/`
    - 美
      - `/'gridi/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 贪婪的
        - `always wanting more food, money, power, possessions etc than you need`
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `greedily`
        - 贪婪地；贪食地
    - n.
      - `greed`
        - 贪婪，贪心
      - `greediness`
        - 贪吃；贪欲；嘴馋
  - 近义词
    - adj.
      - 贪婪的；贪吃的；渴望的
        - `eager`
        - `hungry`
        - `anxious`
        - `desired`
        - `sick`
  - 例句
    - `a greedy and selfish society`
      - 一个贪婪自私的社会
    - `He looked at the gold with greedy eyes.`
      - 他贪婪地看着这些金子。
    - `Have you eaten them all, you greedy pig?`
      - 你把那些全吃了？你这贪吃的猪！
