- umpire
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'ʌmpaɪə/`
    - 美
      - `/'ʌmpaɪɚ/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 当裁判，任裁判
        - `to be the umpire in a game or competition`
    - n.
      - 裁判员，仲裁人
        - `the person who makes sure that the players obey the rules in sports such as tennis, baseball, and cricket`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `umpirage`
        - 仲裁；公断；仲裁人之地位
  - 近义词
    - vi.
      - 当裁判，任裁判
        - `ump`
    - vt.
      - 仲裁，裁判
        - `arbitrate`
    - n.
      - 裁判员，仲裁人
        - `judge`
        - `referee`
  - 例句
    - `He umpired baseball games.`
      - 他过去担任棒球比赛的裁判。
