- beware
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/bɪ'weə/`
    - 美
      - `/bɪ'wɛr/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 小心，谨防
        - `used to warn someone to be careful because something is dangerous`
  - 记忆
    - be(表加强)＋ware(陶器)→陶器易碎， 小心轻放→当心
  - 近义词
    - vi.
      - 当心，小心
        - `take care`
        - `mind your eye`
    - vt.
      - 注意，当心；提防
        - `watching`
        - `take care of`
  - 短语
    - `beware of`
      - 对…小心；谨防… 
  - 例句
    - `Police warned drivers to beware.`
      - 警方提醒司机小心行驶。
