- flighty
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'flaɪtɪ/`
    - 美 `/'flaiti/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 轻浮的；轻狂的；心情浮动的
    - `a woman who is flighty changes her ideas and opinions often, and only remains interested in people or things for a short time`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `flightiness` 疯狂；轻浮
  - 近义词
    - adj. 轻浮的；轻狂的；心情浮动的
      - `flirty`
      - `light-minded`
