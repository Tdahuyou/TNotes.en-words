- incense
  - 发音
    - 英 /'ɪnsens/
    - 美 /'ɪnsɛns/
  - 词义
    - n. 〔燃烧时发出怡人香味的〕香
      - a substance that has a pleasant smell when you burn it
    - v. 使〔某人〕十分愤怒
      - to make someone very angry
  - 记忆
    - in + cens(=cand光亮的) + e → 能发出亮光 → 熏香
  - 同根词
    - adj. incensed 愤怒的，激怒了的
    - v. incensed 激怒（incense的过去分词）
  - 近义词
    - n. 香；奉承
      - court
      - joss stick
  - 短语
    - incense burner 香炉
  - 例句
    - a church filled with the smell of incense 充满焚香气味的教堂
    - lighted incense sticks 点燃的棒香
  - 补充
