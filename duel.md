- duel
  - 发音
    - 英 /'djuːəl/
    - 美 /'dʊəl/
  - 词义
    - n. 决斗；斗争，抗争
      - a fight with weapons between two people, used in the past to settle a quarrel
    - v. 决斗
      - to fight a duel
  - 同根词
    - n. duellist 决斗者
  - 近义词
    - n. 决斗；斗争，抗争
      - war
      - conflict
      - battle
  - 例句
    - The officer challenged him to a duel . 那军官向他提出决斗。
