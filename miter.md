- miter
  - 发音
    - 英 /ˈmaɪtə/
    - 美 /ˈmaɪtər/
  - 词义
    - n. 斜接；斜角；接合角
      - A joint made by cutting two pieces of material (usually at 45 degrees) to fit together, often used in woodworking or framing.
    - v. 使接合成斜角
      - To join two pieces of material at a miter angle, typically at 45 degrees.
  - 记忆
    - miter源自拉丁语"mitra"，意思是头巾，后来引申为两物体在角度上相接合的方式。
  - 同根词
    - n. mitre （与“miter”同义，在某些地区拼写不同）
    - n. mitre saw 斜切锯（用于精确斜接的工具）
  - 近义词
    - n. joint 接头；连接
    - n. corner 角落；角
    - n. angle 角度
  - 短语
    - miter joint 斜接接头
    - miter cut 斜切
    - miter saw 斜切锯
    - miter angle 斜角
    - miter box 斜接盒
  - 例句
    - The carpenter used a miter saw to create precise cuts for the picture frame. 木匠使用斜切锯为相框制作精确的斜接切口。
    - The two pieces of wood were joined at a 45-degree miter joint to form the corner. 这两块木材在45度斜接处接合，形成了角落。
    - For this frame, you’ll need to make a miter cut on both sides. 对于这个框架，你需要在两边都做斜切。
    - The miter angle can be adjusted to suit different types of joints. 斜角可以调整以适应不同类型的接头。
    - A miter box helps guide the saw for accurate miter cuts. 斜接盒有助于引导锯条进行准确的斜切。
