- jail
  - 发音
    - 英 /dʒeɪl/
    - 美 /dʒel/
  - 词义
    - n. 监狱，拘留所
      - a place where criminals are kept as part of their punishment, or where people who have been charged with a crime are kept before they are judged in a law court
    - vt. 监禁某人
      - to put someone in jail
  - 记忆
    - gao(音似： 高)+l(音似： 了)→他因为喝酒喝高了被抓进了拘留所→拘留所
  - 同根词
    - n. jailer 狱卒，看守监狱的人
    - n. jailor 狱卒；看守监狱的人
  - 近义词
    - n. [法]监狱；监牢；拘留所
      - prison
      - big house
    - vt. 监禁；下狱
      - prison
      - imprison
  - 短语
    - in jail 在监狱；在狱中服刑；被封锁了
    - go to jail 入狱
  - 例句
    - He’s been in jail for three months already. 他已经入狱三个月。
  - 补充
