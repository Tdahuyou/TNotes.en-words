- soliloquy
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/sə'liləkwi/`
    - 美
      - `/sə'lɪləkwi/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 自言自语；独白
        - `a speech in a play in which a character, usually alone on the stage, talks to himself or herself so that the  audience  knows their thoughts`
  - 记忆
    - sol (单独) + i + loqu (说) + y → 单独说 → 自言自语
  - 同根词
    - vi.
      - `soliloquize`
        - 自言自语；独白
    - vt.
      - `soliloquize`
        - 自言自语地说
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 独白；自言自语
        - `monologue`
