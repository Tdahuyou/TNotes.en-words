- forthwith
  - 发音
    - 英 /fɔːθ'wɪθ; -ð/
    - 美 /undefined/
  - 词义
    - adv. 立刻，立即；不犹豫地
      - immediately
  - 同根词
    - adj. forthright 直率的；直截了当的
    - adv. forthright 直率地；马上；立即
    - n. forthright 直路
  - 近义词
    - adv. 立刻，立即；不犹豫地
      - rapidly
      - soon
      - shortly
      - directly
      - now
  - 例句
    - He was fined £40, with 28 days’ imprisonment if the money was not produced forthwith. 他被罚款40英镑，如不立即缴付则监禁28天。
