- relentless
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/rɪ'lentlɪs/`
    - 美
      - `/rɪ'lɛntləs/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 无情的；残酷的；不间断的
        - `strict, cruel, or determined, without ever stopping`
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `relentlessly`
        - 残酷地，无情地
  - 近义词
    - adj.
      - 无情的；残酷的；不间断的
        - `cruel`
        - `iron`
        - `ongoing`
        - `ruthless`
  - 例句
    - `her relentless determination to succeed`
      - 她要成功的坚定决心
    - `a regime that was relentless in its persecution of dissidents`
      - 无情迫害持不同政见者的政权
