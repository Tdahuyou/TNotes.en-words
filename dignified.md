- dignified
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'dɪgnɪfaɪd/`
    - 美 `/'dɪɡnɪfaɪd/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 庄严的；有尊严的；高贵的
    - `behaving in a calm and serious way, even in a difficult situation, which makes people respect you`
    - v. 使高贵（dignify的过去式）
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `dignity` 尊严；高贵
    - vt.
      - `dignify` 使高贵；增威严；授以荣誉
  - 近义词
    - adj. 庄严的；有尊严的；高贵的
      - `royal`
      - `solemn`
      - `sacred`
      - `majestic`
      - `distinguished`
  - 例句
    - `a dignified old lady`
      - 庄重的老太太
    - `She made a dignified departure.`
      - 她不失尊严地离去。
