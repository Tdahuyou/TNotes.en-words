- elucidate
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/i'luːsideit/`
    - 美
      - `/ɪ'lusɪdet/`
  - 词义
    - vt.
      - 阐明；说明
        - `to explain something that is difficult to understand by providing more information`
  - 记忆
    - e (出) + lucid (明白的) + ate (做…) → 说出来使明白 → 说明
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 阐明；说明
        - `say`
        - `clarify`
  - 例句
    - `The full picture has not yet been elucidated.`
      - 详细情况还没有解释清楚。
