- wistful
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'wistful/`
    - 美 `/'wɪstfl/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 伤感的，徒然神往的
    - `thinking sadly about something you would like to have but cannot have, especially something that you used to have in the past`
  - 记忆
    - 和wishful (a. 怀有希望的) 一起记
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `wishful` 渴望的，愿望的；寄予希望的
    - adv.
      - `wistfully` 渴望地；希望地；不满足地
      - `wishfully` 希望地，渴望地
    - n.
      - `wish` 希望；祝福；心愿
      - `wistfulness` 渴望；愿望
    - vi.
      - `wish` 愿望；需要
    - vt.
      - `wish` 祝愿；渴望；向…致问候语
  - 近义词
    - adj. 渴望的；留恋的；沉思的
      - `eager`
      - `hungry`
      - `desired`
      - `anxious`
      - `sick`
  - 例句
    - `a wistful smile`
      - 怅然若失的微笑
