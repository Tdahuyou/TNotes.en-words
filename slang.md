- slang
  - 发音
    - 英 `/slæŋ/`
    - 美 `/slæŋ/`
  - 词义
    - n. 俚语；行话
    - `very informal, sometimes offensive language that is used especially by people who belong to a particular group, such as young people or criminals`
    - adj. 俚语的
    - v. 用粗话骂
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `slangy` 俚语的；俗话多的；好用俚语的
  - 近义词
    - n. 俚语；行话
      - `jargon`
      - `dialect`
  - 例句
    - `schoolboy slang`
      - 学生俚语
