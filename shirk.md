- shirk
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ʃɜːrk/`
    - 美
      - `/ʃə:k/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 逃避，规避
        - `to deliberately avoid doing something you should do, because you are lazy`
  - 记忆
    - 通过假笑(smirk)来逃避(shirk)责任
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `shirker`
        - 懒骨头；逃避责任者；开小差的人
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 逃避
        - `escape`
        - `get out of`
    - vi.
      - 推卸；逃避
        - `shift off`
        - `shuffle off`
  - 例句
    - `He was fired for shirking.`
      - 他因偷懒被解雇了。
