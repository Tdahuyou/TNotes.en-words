- famine
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'fæmɪn/`
    - 美
      - `/'fæmɪn/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 饥荒,
        - `a situation in which a large number of people have little or no food for a long time and many people die`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `famished`
        - 极饥饿的
    - v.
      - `famished`
        - 使饥饿（famish的过去式）
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 饥荒；饥饿，奇缺
        - `hunger`
        - `fames`
  - 例句
    - `the great potato famine in Ireland`
      - 爱尔兰严重的土豆荒
    - `A million people are facing famine .`
      - 一百万人正面临饥荒。
