- procrastinate
  - 发音
    - 英 `/prəu'kræstineit/`
    - 美 `/pro'kræstɪnet/`
  - 词义
    - v. 延迟，耽搁
    - `to delay doing something that you ought to do, usually because you do not want to do it`
  - 记忆
    - pro (在前) + crastin (明天) + ate (使) → 今朝有酒今朝醉， 勿到明日无酒喝 → 延迟， 耽搁
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `procrastination` 耽搁，拖延
      - `procrastinator` 拖延者，拖拉者；因循者
  - 近义词
    - vt. 耽搁，延迟
      - `time delay`
      - `detain`
    - vi. 耽搁，延迟
      - `wait`
      - `time delay`
  - 例句
    - `People often procrastinate when it comes to paperwork.`
      - 遇到文书工作，人们常常会拖拖拉拉。
