- supermarket
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'suːpəmɑːkɪt; 'sjuː-/`
    - 美
      - `/'sʊpɚ'mɑrkɪt/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 超市
        - `a very large shop that sells food, drinks, and things that people need regularly in their homes`
  - 记忆
    - super(超级)+market(市场)→超市
  - 例句
    - `Most of us do our food shopping in the supermarket.`
      - 我们大多数人在这家超市采购食品。
