- premonition
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ˌpremə'niʃən/`
    - 美
      - `/,prɛmə'nɪʃən/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 预先的警告；预感，前兆
        - `a strange feeling that something, especially something bad, is going to happen`
  - 记忆
    - pre (预先) + monit (警告) + ion → 预先的警告
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 预告；征兆
        - `symptom`
        - `preliminary notice`
  - 例句
    - `He had an unshakable premonition that he would die.`
      - 他有一种强烈的预感：他就要死了。
