- conducive
  - 发音
    - 英 /kən'duːsɪv/
    - 美 /kən'dusɪv/
  - 词义
    - adj. 有助于…的，有益于…的
      - if a situation is conducive to something such as work, rest etc, it provides conditions that make it easy for you to work etc
  - 记忆
    - 来自conduce(v. 导致； 有益于)
  - 同根词
    - n. conduciveness 促成；诱因
    - vi. conduce 导致；有益，有贡献于
  - 近义词
    - adj. 有益的；有助于…的
      - beneficial
      - helpful
      - useful
      - advantageous
      - profitable
  - 例句
    - an environment conducive to learning 有助于学习的环境
  - 补充
