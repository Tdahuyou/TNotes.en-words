- shaman
  - 发音
    - 英 /'ʃæmən; 'ʃeɪm-/
    - 美 /'ʃæmən/
  - 词义
    - n. 萨满教的道士,僧人或巫师
      - a person in some tribes who is a religious leader and is believed to be able to talk to  spirits  and cure illnesses
  - 同根词
    - n. shamanism 萨满教；黄教
    - n. shamanist 黄教徒；萨满教徒
  - 近义词
    - n. 巫师；萨满教的道士；僧人
      - necromancer
      - koradji
  - 补充
