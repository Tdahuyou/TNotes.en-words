- congratulation
  - 发音
    - 英 `/kənˌgrætʃu'leɪʃn/`
    - 美 `/kən,ɡrætʃu'leʃən/`
  - 词义
    - n. 祝贺，道喜
    - `when you tell someone that you are happy because they have achieved something or because something nice has happened to them`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `congratulatory` 祝贺的；庆祝的
    - vt.
      - `congratulate` 祝贺；恭喜；庆贺
  - 近义词
    - n. 祝贺；贺辞
      - `felicitation`
      - `gala`
  - 例句
    - `letters of congratulation`
      - 祝贺信
