- suitcase
  - 发音
    - 英 /'suːtkeɪs/
    - 美 /'sutkes/
  - 词义
    - n. 手提箱，衣箱
      - a large case with a handle, used for carrying clothes and possessions when you travel
  - 记忆
    - suit(一套衣服) + case(箱子) → 衣箱
  - 近义词
    - n. [轻]手提箱；衣箱
      - carrying case
      - hold-all
  - 例句
    - It did not take Andrew long to pack a suitcase. 安德鲁没有用多长时间就整理好了手提箱。
  - 补充
