- retouch
  - 发音
    - 英 /ˌriː'tʌtʃ/
    - 美 /,ri'tʌtʃ/
  - 词义
    - v. 润饰
      - to improve a picture or photograph by painting over marks or making other small changes
  - 记忆
    - re + touch (用画笔轻画) → 一再用画笔轻画 → 润饰
  - 近义词
    - vt. 润饰；修整
      - trim up
      - give shape to
    - vi. 润饰；修整
      - trim up
      - give shape to
  - 例句
    - postcards that have been retouched to cover the grey skies 经过修饰把灰色天空掩盖起来的明信片
  - 补充
