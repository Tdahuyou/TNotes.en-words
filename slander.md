- slander
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'slændər/`
    - 美
      - `/'slændɚ/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 诽谤，中伤，诋毁
        - `a false spoken statement about someone, intended to damage the good opinion that people have of that person`
    - vt.
      - 诽谤，诋毁
        - `to say false things about someone in order to damage other people’s good opinion of them`
  - 记忆
    - sland(=scandal障碍物) + er → 设障碍物， 使人绊倒 → 诽谤， 诋毁
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `slanderous`
        - 诽谤的；诽谤性的；中伤的
    - adv.
      - `slanderously`
        - 诽谤地；造谣中伤地；恶毒地
    - n.
      - `slanderer`
        - 诽谤者
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 诽谤；造谣中伤
        - `wrong`
        - `asperse`
    - n.
      - 诽谤；中伤
        - `defamation`
        - `scandal`
  - 例句
    - `He is being sued for slander .`
      - 他被控犯有口头诽谤罪。
