- percolate
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'pɜːrkəleɪt/`
    - 美
      - `/'pɝkəlet/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 过滤；渗入，渗透；逐渐流传，传开
        - `if an idea, feeling, or piece of information percolates through a group, it gradually spreads`
    - n.
      - 滤液
  - 记忆
    - per(贯穿) + col(过滤) + ate → 过滤； 渗入， 渗透
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `percolation`
        - 过滤；浸透
      - `percolator`
        - 过滤器；渗滤器；咖啡渗滤壶
  - 近义词
    - vi.
      - 过滤；渗出；浸透
        - `filtrate`
        - `water logging`
    - vt.
      - 使渗出；使过滤
        - `exude`
    - n.
      - 滤过液；渗出液
        - `transduate`
        - `diffusate`
  - 例句
    - `These ideas were slow to percolate.`
      - 这些观念流传得很慢。
