- recite
  - 发音
    - 英 `/rɪ'saɪt/`
    - 美 `/rɪ'saɪt/`
  - 词义
    - v. 背诵；叙述；列举
    - `to say a poem, piece of literature etc that you have learned, for people to listen to`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `recitative` 吟诵的；背诵的；叙述的
    - n.
      - `recital` 朗诵，吟诵；独奏会；背诵；独唱会
      - `recitation` 背诵；朗诵；详述；背诵的诗
      - `recitative` 朗诵调；宣叙调
      - `reciter` 朗诵者；背诵者
  - 近义词
    - vt. 背诵；叙述；列举
      - `repeat`
      - `specify`
      - `relate`
    - vi. 背诵；叙述
      - `say over`
      - `learn sth by memory`
  - 例句
    - `She recited a poem that she had learnt at school.`
      - 她背诵了一首学校里学的诗。
