- imperious
  - 发音
    - 英 `/im'piəriəs/`
    - 美 `/ɪm'pɪrɪəs/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 专横的
    - `giving orders and expecting to be obeyed, in a way that seems too proud`
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `imperiously` 专制地；妄自尊大地
  - 近义词
    - adj. 专横的；迫切的；傲慢的
      - `pressing`
      - `stringent`
  - 例句
    - `She raised her hand in an imperious gesture.`
      - 她傲慢地扬起手。
