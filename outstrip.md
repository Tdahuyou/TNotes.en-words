- outstrip
  - 发音
    - 英 /ˌaut'strip/
    - 美 /,aʊt'strɪp/
  - 词义
    - v. 超过
      - to do something better than someone else or be more successful
  - 记忆
    - out (超过) + strip (条； 带) → 超过
  - 近义词
    - vt. 超过；胜过；比…跑得快
      - better than
      - overcome
      - top
      - cap
      - outdo
  - 例句
    - We outstripped all our competitors in sales last year. 去年我们的销售额超过了所有竞争对手。
  - 补充
