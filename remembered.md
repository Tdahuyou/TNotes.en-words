- remembered
  - 发音
    - 英 /rɪˈmembəd/
    - 美 /rɪˈmembərd/
  - 词义
    - v. 记得；想起；纪念（remember 的过去式和过去分词）
      - Recalled information or experiences from memory; kept in mind.
  - 同根词
    - v. remember 记得；纪念
    - adj. memorable 难忘的
    - n. remembrance 纪念；回忆
    - adj. remembered 被记住的
    - n. rememberer 记得的人
  - 近义词
    - v. 记得
      - recalled
      - recollected
      - retained
      - commemorated
    - v. 想起
      - reminisced
      - evoked
      - summoned
  - 短语
    - fondly remembered 深情回忆
    - remembered as 被铭记为
    - suddenly remembered 突然想起
    - remembered to 记得去做
    - best remembered for 以...闻名于世
  - 例句
    - She suddenly remembered turning off the stove before leaving. 她突然想起出门前关掉了炉灶。
    - The soldiers are remembered for their bravery in the memorial ceremony. 在纪念仪式上，士兵们的英勇被铭记。
    - He remembered to send his mother flowers on her birthday. 他记得在母亲生日时送花。
    - The city remembered the earthquake victims with a minute of silence. 全市以一分钟默哀纪念地震遇难者。
    - Maya Angelou is best remembered for her autobiography "I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings". 玛雅·安杰卢以自传《我知道笼中鸟为何歌唱》闻名于世。
  - 补充
    - 描述对过去事件的有意识回忆，既包括个人经历（remembered turning off），也指集体记忆（remembered for bravery）。在心理学中与"recall"同属记忆提取过程，文化领域指历史纪念（remembered events）。常与情感副词连用（fondly/vividly/suddenly remembered）。
