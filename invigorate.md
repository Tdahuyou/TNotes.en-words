- invigorate
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ɪn'vɪgəreɪt/`
    - 美 `/ɪn'vɪɡəret/`
  - 词义
    - v. 鼓舞
    - `to make the people in an organization or group feel excited again, so that they want to make something successful`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `invigorating` 爽快的；精力充沛的
      - `invigorated` 精力充沛的；生气勃勃的
    - n.
      - `invigoration` 鼓舞；身心愉快；精力充沛；滋补
      - `invigorator` 强壮剂，补药
    - v.
      - `invigorated` 鼓舞；增添活力（invigorate的过去式）
    - vt.
      - `invigorating` 振兴（invigorate的ing形式）
  - 近义词
    - vt. 鼓舞；使精力充沛
      - `stimulate`
      - `lift`
      - `spirit`
      - `inspire`
  - 例句
    - `Carey’s hope was that the church would be renewed and invigorated.`
      - 凯里希望教会能复兴。
