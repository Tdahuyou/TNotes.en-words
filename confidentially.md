- confidentially
  - 发音
    - 英 `/,kɒnfɪ'denʃ(ə)lɪ/`
    - 美 `/,kɔnfi'denʃəli/`
  - 词义
    - adv. 秘密地；作为心腹话地
    - `Confidentially is used to say that what you are telling someone is a secret and should not be discussed with anyone else`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `confidence` （美）诈骗的；骗得信任的
      - `confidential` 机密的；表示信任的；获信任的
    - n.
      - `confidence` 信心；信任；秘密
      - `confidentiality` 机密，[计] 机密性
    - vi.
      - `confide` 信赖；吐露秘密
    - vt.
      - `confide` 吐露；委托
  - 近义词
    - adv. 秘密地；作为心腹话地
      - `quietly`
      - `underground`
      - `secretly`
      - `privately`
  - 例句
    - `Confidentially, I am not sure that it wasn't above their heads.`
      - 私下里说，我不确定这没有超出他们的理解。
