- confiscate
  - 发音
    - 英 /'kɑːnfɪskeɪt/
    - 美 /'kɑnfɪsket/
  - 词义
    - vt. 没收，充公，征用
      - to officially take private property away from someone, usually as a punishment
  - 记忆
    - con(和) + fisc(箱； 金库) + ate → 使和金库分开 → 没收， 充公， 征用
  - 同根词
    - n. confiscation 没收；征用；充公
  - 近义词
    - vt. 没收；充公；查抄
      - sequestrate
      - expropriate
    - adj. 被没收的
      - forfeit
  - 例句
    - Miss Williams confiscated all our sweets. 威廉斯老师没收了我们所有的糖果。
    - Many opposition supporters had their goods confiscated. 许多反对党支持者的财产被充公了。
