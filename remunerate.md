- remunerate
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ri'mjuːnəˌreit/`
    - 美 `/rɪ'mjunəret/`
  - 词义
    - v. 酬劳
    - `If you are remunerated for work that you do, you are paid for it`
  - 记忆
    - re + muner (礼物) + ate → 千里送鹅毛， 礼轻情义重 → 酬劳
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `remunerative` 有利可图的；有报酬的；合算的
      - `remunerated` 获得酬金的
    - n.
      - `remuneration` 报酬；酬劳，赔偿
      - `remunerator` 酬劳者
    - v.
      - `remunerated` 付给…报酬（remunerate的过去式）
  - 近义词
    - vt. 酬劳；给与报酬；赔偿
      - `make good`
      - `indemnify`
  - 例句
    - `You will be remunerated and so will your staff.`
      - 你将会得到报酬，你的员工也会。
