- supplicant
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'sʌplikənt/`
    - 美
      - `/'sʌplɪkənt/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 恳求者，恳请者
        - `someone who asks for something, especially from someone in a position of power or from God`
  - 记忆
    - 来自supplicate (v. 恳求， 恳请)
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `suppliant`
        - 恳求的；哀求的
      - `supplicatory`
        - 恳求的；祈求的
    - n.
      - `suppliant`
        - 恳求者；哀求者
      - `supplication`
        - 恳求；祈祷
    - vi.
      - `supplicate`
        - 哀求；恳请；祈求
    - vt.
      - `supplicate`
        - 恳求，哀求；恳请
  - 例句
    - `He flung himself down in the flat submissive posture of a mere supplicant.`
      - 他猛地趴在地上，做出一个卑微的哀求者那谦卑的姿态。
