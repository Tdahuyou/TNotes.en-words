- indemnify
  - 发音
    - 英 /ɪn'demnɪfaɪ/
    - 美 /ɪn'dɛmnɪfaɪ/
  - 词义
    - v. 赔偿；保护；使免于受罚
      - to promise to pay someone if something they own is damaged or lost
  - 同根词
    - n. indemnification 赔偿；保护；赦免；补偿金
  - 近义词
    - vt. 赔偿；保护；使免于受罚
      - secure
      - preserve
      - make good
  - 例句
    - They agreed to indemnify the taxpayers against any loss. 他们同意赔偿纳税人的任何损失。
  - 补充
