- banish
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'bænɪʃ/`
    - 美 `/'bænɪʃ/`
  - 词义
    - v. 放逐；驱逐
    - `to not allow someone or something to stay in a particular place`
    - n. (Banish)人名；(英)巴尼什
  - 近义词
    - vt. 放逐；驱逐
      - `ostracize`
      - `expel`
  - 例句
    - `John was banished from England.`
      - 约翰被逐出英国。
