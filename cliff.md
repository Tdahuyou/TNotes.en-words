- cliff
  - 发音
    - 英 `/klɪf/`
    - 美 `/klɪf/`
  - 词义
    - n. 悬崖，峭壁
    - `a large area of rock or a mountain with a very steep side, often at the edge of the sea or a river`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `cliffy` 陡峭的；多悬崖的
  - 近义词
    - n. [地理]悬崖；绝壁
      - `precipice`
      - `escarpment`
  - 短语
    - `red cliff` 赤壁 
  - 例句
    - `The car rolled over the edge of a cliff.`
      - 汽车翻下了悬崖边。
