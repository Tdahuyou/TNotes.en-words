- humiliation
  - 发音
    - 英 `/hju:ˌmɪlɪ'eɪʃn/`
    - 美 `/hjʊ,mɪlɪ'eʃən/`
  - 词义
    - n. 丢脸
    - `a feeling of shame and great embarrassment, because you have been made to look stupid or weak`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `humiliated` 羞辱的
      - `humiliating` 丢脸的；羞辱性的
    - n.
      - `humility` 谦卑，谦逊
    - v.
      - `humiliated` 屈辱（humiliate的过去式）；使…丢脸，使…蒙羞
      - `humiliating` 使蒙耻（humiliate的ing形式）
    - vt.
      - `humiliate` 羞辱；使…丢脸；耻辱
  - 近义词
    - n. 丢脸，耻辱；蒙羞；谦卑
      - `lost face`
      - `stigma`
  - 例句
    - `the humiliation of having to ask her parents for money`
      - 不得不开口伸手向她父母要钱的难堪
