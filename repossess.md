- repossess
  - 发音
    - 英 /,riːpə'zes/
    - 美 /,ripə'zɛs/
  - 词义
    - v. 收回,复得
      - to take back cars, furniture, or property from people who had arranged to pay for them over a long time, but cannot now continue to pay for them
  - 同根词
    - n. repossession 恢复；取回
  - 近义词
    - vt. 收回；取复；重新获得
      - recover
      - withdraw
      - resume
  - 例句
    - Eventually the bailiffs came to repossess the flat. 查封官终于来收公寓了。
  - 补充
