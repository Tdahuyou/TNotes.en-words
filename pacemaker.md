- pacemaker
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'peɪsmeɪkə/`
    - 美
      - `/'pesmekɚ/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - [基医]起搏器；领跑者；标兵
        - `a small machine that is placed inside someone’s chest in order to help their heart beat regularly`
  - 记忆
    - pace(速度) + maker(制造者) → 领跑者
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `pacesetter`
        - 标兵；先导者，步调调整者
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - [基医][解剖]起搏器；领跑者；标兵
        - `frontrunner`
  - 短语
    - `cardiac pacemaker`
      - 心脏起搏器 
  - 例句
    - `She was fitted with a pacemaker after suffering serious heart trouble.`
      - 她在心脏出了严重问题后装上了心脏起搏器。
