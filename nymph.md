- nymph
  - 发音
    - 英 `/nɪmf/`
    - 美 `/nɪmf/`
  - 词义
    - n. 女神；居于山林水泽的仙女；美丽的少女；蛹
    - `one of the spirits of nature who, according to ancient Greek and Roman stories, appeared as young girls living in trees, mountains, streams etc`
  - 近义词
    - n. 女神；居于山林水泽的仙女；美丽的少女；蛹
      - `goddess`
      - `pupa`
