- primp
  - 发音
    - 英 /primp/
    - 美 /prɪmp/
  - 词义
    - v. 细心打扮，过分装饰
      - to make yourself look attractive by arranging your hair, putting on  make-up  etc
  - 记忆
    - prim (最早， 最好) + p → 向最好处打扮 → 细心打扮
  - 近义词
    - vt. 精心打扮；装饰
      - paint
      - hang
    - vi. 装饰；打扮
      - dress oneself
      - sharp
      - trick out
  - 例句
    - She spends hours primping in front of the mirror. 她总是花很长时间在镜子前梳妆打扮。
  - 补充
