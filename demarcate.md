- demarcate
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'diːmɑːrkeɪt/`
    - 美
      - `/'dimɑr'ket/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 划分，划界
        - `If you demarcate something, you establish its boundaries or limits`
  - 记忆
    - de + marc(=mark标记) + ate → 做标记 → 划分， 划界
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `demarcation`
        - 划分；划界；限界
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 划分界线；区别
        - `to make a distinction`
        - `tell from`
  - 例句
    - `A special UN commission was formed to demarcate the border.`
      - 一个特别联合国委员会被设立以期划定边界。
