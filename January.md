- January
  - 发音
    - 英 /'dʒænjʊ(ə)rɪ/
    - 美 /'dʒænjʊ'ɛri/
  - 词义
    - n. 一月
      - the first month of the year, between December and February
  - 短语
    - in january 在一月
  - 例句
    - We always have snow in January. 我们这里1月份总会下雪。
