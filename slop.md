- slop
  - 发音
    - 英 /slɒp/
    - 美 /slɑp/
  - 词义
    - v. 溢出,溅溢
      - if liquid slops somewhere, it moves around or over the edge of a container in an uncontrolled way
  - 同根词
    - adj. sloping 倾斜的；有坡度的；成斜坡的
    - v. sloping 溢出（slop的ing形式）
  - 近义词
    - vt. 溢出，使溅出
      - flood
      - spill over
    - vi. 溅出；泼洒
      - spill out
  - 例句
    - Coffee slopped over the rim of her cup. 咖啡从她的杯口溢了出来。
    - With each wave, more water slopped into the cabin. 随着一个个浪头打来，更多的水漫进了船舱。
  - 补充
