- diaper
  - 发音
    - 英 /'daɪpər/
    - 美 /'daɪpɚ/
  - 词义
    - n. 尿布
      - a piece of soft cloth or soft paper that is put between a baby’s legs and fastened around its waist to hold liquid and solid waste
  - 记忆
    - di(分开) + aper(看作paper纸) → 能把水分隔开的纸 → 尿布
  - 近义词
    - n. 尿布
      - didy
      - nappy
  - 短语
    - diaper rash [医]尿布疹
    - diaper dermatitis [医]尿布皮炎；[医]尿布红斑
    - disposable diaper 一次性尿片，纸尿裤
  - 例句
    - I changed her diaper . 我给她换了尿布。
  - 补充
