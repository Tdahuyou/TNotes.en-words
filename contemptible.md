- contemptible
  - 发音
    - 英 /kən'temptəbl/
    - 美 /kən'tɛmptəbl/
  - 词义
    - adj. 可鄙的
      - not deserving any respect at all
  - 记忆
    - 来自contempt (n. 轻视)
  - 同根词
    - adv. contemptibly 卑鄙地；下贱地
    - n. contempt 轻视，蔑视；耻辱
    - n. contemptibility 可鄙；卑鄙；下贱
  - 近义词
    - adj. 可鄙的；卑劣的；可轻视的
      - despicable
      - underhanded
  - 例句
    - They were portrayed as contemptible cowards. 他们被刻画成可鄙的胆小鬼。
  - 补充
