- amputate
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'æmpjuteit/`
    - 美
      - `/'æmpjutet/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 切除
        - `to cut off someone’s arm, leg, finger etc during a medical operation`
  - 记忆
    - am (看作arm) + put (切除) + ate (做) → 切除 (手臂、 腿等)
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `amputation`
        - 截肢；切断（术）
      - `amputee`
        - 被截肢者
      - `amputator`
        - 切断者；切断器
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 截肢；切断；删除
        - `cut off`
        - `cut out`
  - 例句
    - `Two of her toes were amputated because of frostbite.`
      - 她的两个脚趾因为冻伤被截掉了。
