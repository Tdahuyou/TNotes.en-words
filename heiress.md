- heiress
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'eərɪs; eə'res/`
    - 美 `/'εəris/`
  - 词义
    - n. 女继承人
    - `a woman who will receive or has received a lot of money or property after the death of an older member of her family`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `heir` [法] 继承人；后嗣；嗣子
  - 近义词
    - n. 女继承人
      - `inheritress`
  - 例句
    - `...the heiress to a jewellery empire.`
      - …一家大型珠宝公司的女继承人。
