- northeast
  - 发音
    - 英 `/,nɔ:θ'i:st/`
    - 美 `/'nɔrθ'ist/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 在东北的
    - `The northeast edge, corner, or part of a place is the part which is toward the northeast`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `northeasterly` 向东北的；来自东北的
      - `northeastward` 向东北的
      - `northerly` 北方的，向北的；来自北方的
    - adv.
      - `northeasterly` 向东北；来自东北
      - `northeastward` 向东北；在东北
      - `northerly` 向北；来自北方
    - n.
      - `northeaster` 东北风；东北的暴风
      - `northeastward` 东北
      - `northerly` 北风
  - 例句
    - `...a climate like that of our northeast coast.`
      - …同我们东北部沿海气候相似的一种气候。
