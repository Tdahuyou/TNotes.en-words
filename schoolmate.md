- schoolmate
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'skuːlmeɪt/`
    - 美
      - `/'skʊl'met/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 同学；校友
        - `someone who goes or went to the same school as you`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `schoolfellow`
        - 校友；同学
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 同学；同窗
        - `condisciple`
        - `kollegen`
  - 例句
    - `He started the magazine with a schoolmate.`
      - 他和他的一个同学创办了那份杂志。
