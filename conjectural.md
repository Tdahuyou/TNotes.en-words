- conjectural
  - 发音
    - 英 `/kən'dʒektʃ(ə)r(ə)l/`
    - 美 `/kən'dʒɛktʃərəl/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 推测的,好推测的
    - `A statement that is conjectural is based on information that is not certain or complete`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `conjecture` 推测；猜想
    - vi.
      - `conjecture` 推测；揣摩
    - vt.
      - `conjecture` 推测
  - 近义词
    - adj. 推测的；好推测的
      - `speculative`
      - `extrapolated`
  - 例句
    - `There is something undeniably conjectural about such claims.`
      - 此类声明中绝对有推测的成分。
