- dinghy
  - 发音
    - 英 /'dɪŋgɪ; 'dɪŋɪ/
    - 美 /dɪŋi/
  - 词义
    - n. 小艇；小船
      - a small open boat used for pleasure, or for taking people between a ship and the shore
  - 近义词
    - n. 小艇；小船
      - boat
      - tender
  - 短语
    - rubber dinghy 橡皮艇
  - 例句
    - ...a rubber dinghy. …一条橡皮艇。
  - 补充
