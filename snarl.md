- snarl
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/snɑːl/`
    - 美
      - `/snɑrl/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 咆哮；怒骂；混乱
        - `a vicious growl, utterance, or facial expression `
    - v.
      - 咆哮；怒骂；缠结
        - `if an animal snarls, it makes a low angry sound and shows its teeth`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `snarly`
        - 纠缠在一起的；善于嚣叫的；爱漫骂的；脾气坏的
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 咆哮；怒骂；混乱
        - `chaos`
        - `disorder`
        - `confusion`
        - `involvement`
        - `mix`
    - vi.
      - 咆哮；怒骂；缠结
        - `gnar`
        - `huff and puff`
    - vt.
      - 搞乱；咆哮着说；使…缠结
        - `gum up`
        - `bolix`
  - 例句
    - `‘Shut up,’ he snarled.`
      - “住口。”他怒吼道。
