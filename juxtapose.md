- juxtapose
  - 发音
    - 英 /ˌdʒʌkstə'pəuz/
    - 美 /'dʒʌkstə'poz/
  - 词义
    - v. 并置，并列
      - to put things together, especially things that are not normally together, in order to compare them or to make something new
  - 记忆
    - juxta (接近) + pose (放) → 紧挨着放 → 并置， 并列
  - 同根词
    - n. juxtaposition 并置，并列；毗邻
  - 近义词
    - vt. 并列；并置
      - counterpose
  - 例句
    - The technique Mr. Wilson uses most often is to juxtapose things for dramatic effect. 威尔逊先生最常用的技巧是把事物并列起来以获得戏剧性效果。
