- surmise
  - 发音
    - 英 /sə'maɪz/
    - 美 /sɝ'maɪz/
  - 词义
    - v. 推测，猜测
      - to guess that something is true, using the information you know already
    - n. 推测，猜测
      - If you say that a particular conclusion is surmise, you mean that it is a guess based on the available evidence and you do not know for certain that it is true
  - 记忆
    - sur(在…之上) + mis(放) + e → 放在事情之上 → 推测， 猜测
  - 同根词
    - adj. surmisable 可以推测得出的，可揣度的
  - 近义词
    - vt. 猜测；推测
      - make a guess at
      - guess at sth
    - vi. 猜测；认为
      - consider
      - guess at sth
    - n. 推测；猜度
      - speculation
      - theory
      - guess
      - projection
  - 例句
    - There's so little to go on, we can only surmise what happened. 几乎没有什么可继续进行的，我们只能猜测发生过的事。
    - It is mere surmise that Bosch had Brant's poem in mind when doing this painting. 博斯画这幅画时头脑里想着勃朗特的诗，这只是猜测而已。
