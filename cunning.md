- cunning
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'kʌnɪŋ/`
    - 美 `/'kʌnɪŋ/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 善于骗人的，狡猾的
    - `someone who is cunning is clever and good at deceiving people in order to get what they want`
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `cunningly` 巧妙地，狡猾地
  - 近义词
    - adj. 狡猾的；巧妙的；可爱的
      - `happy`
      - `lovely`
      - `designing`
      - `knowing`
      - `pretty`
    - n. 狡猾
      - `artfulness`
      - `subtility`
  - 例句
    - `a cunning opponent`
      - 狡猾的对手
