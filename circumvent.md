- circumvent
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/sɜːkəm'vent/`
    - 美
      - `/,sɝkəm'vɛnt/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 绕行,设法避开
        - `to avoid something by changing the direction in which you are travelling`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `circumvention`
        - 规避；陷害；绕行
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 包围；陷害；绕行
        - `case`
        - `invest`
        - `frame`
  - 例句
    - `We went north in order to circumvent the mountains.`
      - 我们往北走以绕开这些山。
