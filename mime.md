- mime
  - 发音
    - 英 /maɪm/
    - 美 /maɪm/
  - 词义
    - v. 模拟，模仿
      - to pretend to play or sing a piece of music, without making any sound
    - n. 喜剧；哑剧；哑剧演员；模拟表演
      - the use of movements to express what you want to say without using words, or a play where the actors use only movements
  - 记忆
    - mim(模仿) + e → 模拟
  - 同根词
    - adj. mimic 模仿的，模拟的；假装的
    - n. mimic 效颦者，模仿者；仿制品；小丑
    - vt. mimic 模仿，摹拟
  - 近义词
    - n. 哑剧；小丑；滑稽戏
      - dumb show
      - panto
  - 例句
    - The children learn through role-play, dance and mime. 儿童通过角色表演、舞蹈和哑剧来学习。
    - They will perform a short mime later. 稍后他们将表演一个哑剧小品。
    - a professional mime artist 专业的哑剧表演艺术家
