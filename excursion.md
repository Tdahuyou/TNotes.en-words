- excursion
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ɪk'skɜːrʒn/`
    - 美
      - `/ɪk'skɝʒn/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 远足，短途旅游
        - `a short journey arranged so that a group of people can visit a place, especially while they are on holiday`
  - 记忆
    - ex(出， 外) + curs(跑) + ion → 跑出去 → 远足
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 偏移；远足；短程旅行；离题；游览圆
        - `skewing`
        - `short trip`
  - 短语
    - `temperature excursion`
      - 温度剧增，超温 
  - 例句
    - `a shopping excursion`
      - 购物游
