- entomb
  - 发音
    - 英 /ɪn'tuːm; en-/
    - 美 /ɪn'tum/
  - 词义
    - v. 埋葬,成为的坟墓
      - to bury or trap someone in something or under the ground
  - 同根词
    - n. entombment 埋葬；埋没
  - 近义词
    - vt. 埋葬；成为…的坟墓
      - bury
      - tomb
  - 例句
    - The city was entombed in volcanic lava. 那个城市被火山熔岩掩埋了。
  - 补充
