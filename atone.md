- atone
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ə'təun/`
    - 美
      - `/ə'təun/`
  - 词义
    - vi.
      - 弥补
        - `to do something to show that you are sorry for having done something wrong`
  - 记忆
    - a + tone (看作stone， 石头) → 女娲以石补天 → 弥补
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `atonement`
        - 赎罪；补偿，弥补
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 赎罪；弥补；偿还
        - `replace`
        - `recover`
        - `offset`
        - `tender`
    - vi.
      - 弥补；赎回
        - `make up for it`
        - `make up for sth.`
  - 短语
    - `atone for`
      - 赎回；抵偿 
  - 例句
    - `He felt he had atoned for what he had done to his son.`
      - 他觉得他已弥补曾对儿子所做的错事。
