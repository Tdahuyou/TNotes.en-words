- stupor
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'stjuːpə/`
    - 美
      - `/'stupɚ/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 昏迷
        - `a state in which you cannot think, speak, see, or hear clearly, usually because you have drunk too much alcohol or taken drugs`
  - 记忆
    - stup (看作stop， 停止) + or → 停止活动 → 昏迷
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `stupid`
        - 愚蠢的；麻木的；乏味的
      - `stuporous`
        - 昏迷的，不省人事的；无感觉的
    - n.
      - `stupid`
        - 傻瓜，笨蛋
      - `stupefaction`
        - 麻醉；昏迷；麻木状态
    - vt.
      - `stupefy`
        - 使惊呆；使昏迷
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 昏迷，恍惚；麻木
        - `coma`
        - `exanimation`
  - 例句
    - `We found him lying at the bottom of the stairs in a drunken stupor .`
      - 我们发现他躺在楼梯口，醉得不省人事。
