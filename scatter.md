- scatter
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'skætər/`
    - 美 `/'skætɚ/`
  - 词义
    - v. 使分散，驱散，散开；撒播
    - `if someone scatters a lot of things, or if they scatter, they are thrown or dropped over a wide area in an irregular way`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `scattered` 分散的；散乱的
      - `scattering` 分散的
      - `scattershot` 漫无目标的；任意的
    - int.
      - `scat` 嘘（赶猫等的呼声）
    - n.
      - `scattering` 散射；分散
      - `scat` 拟声唱法
      - `scattershot` 散射
    - v.
      - `scattering` 散射；散布；驱散（scatter的ing形式）
    - vi.
      - `scat` 用拟声唱法唱；走开
  - 近义词
    - vi. 分散，散开；[军]散射
      - `spread around`
      - `spread about`
    - vt. [军]使散射；使散开，使分散；使散播，使撒播
      - `disject`
      - `disperse`
    - n. 分散；散播，撒播
      - `dissemination`
      - `decentralization`
  - 例句
    - `The sound of gunfire made the crowd scatter in all directions.`
      - 枪声使人群四散奔逃。
