- debrief
  - 发音
    - 英 /ˌdiː'briːf/
    - 美 /di'brif/
  - 词义
    - v. 盘问，听取报告
      - to ask someone questions about a job they have just done or an experience they have just had, in order to gather information
  - 记忆
    - de + brief(简述) → 听取报告
  - 同根词
    - n. debriefing 任务报告；任务报告中提出的情报
  - 近义词
    - vt. 盘问；听取报告
      - cross examination
      - catechize
  - 例句
    - The returning bomber crews were debriefed. 询问了返回的轰炸机机组人员以了解情况。
  - 补充
