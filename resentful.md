- resentful
  - 发音
    - 英 /rɪ'zentfʊl; -f(ə)l/
    - 美 /rɪ'zɛntfl/
  - 词义
    - adj. 充满忿恨的；厌恶的
      - feeling angry and upset about something that you think is unfair
  - 同根词
    - adv. resentfully 充满愤恨地
    - n. resentment 愤恨，怨恨
    - vt. resent 怨恨；愤恨；厌恶
  - 近义词
    - adj. 充满忿恨的；厌恶的
      - sick
      - laith
  - 例句
    - At first I felt very resentful and angry about losing my job. 起初,我对丢掉工作感到非常怨恨和恼怒。
