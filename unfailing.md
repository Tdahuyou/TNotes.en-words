- unfailing
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ʌn'feɪlɪŋ/`
    - 美 `/ʌn'felɪŋ/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 经久不衰的；可靠的；无穷尽的
    - `always there, even in times of difficulty or trouble`
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `unfailingly` 可靠地；无穷尽地；不倦地
  - 近义词
    - adj. 经久不衰的；可靠的；无穷尽的
      - `responsible`
      - `honest`
      - `reliable`
      - `tried`
      - `safe`
  - 例句
    - `She battled against cancer with unfailing good humour.`
      - 她始终乐观地与癌症抗争。
