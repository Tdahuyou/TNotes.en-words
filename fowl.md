- fowl
  - 发音
    - 英 /faʊl/
    - 美 /faʊl/
  - 词义
    - n. 家禽；鸟；飞禽
      - a bird, such as a chicken, that is kept for its meat and eggs, or the meat of this type of bird
    - v. 打鸟；捕野禽
  - 同根词
    - n. fowler 捕野禽者
  - 近义词
    - n. [禽]家禽；鸟；飞禽
      - bird
      - poultry
  - 短语
    - silky fowl 乌骨鸡
    - guinea fowl n. 珍珠鸡
  - 例句
    - Carve the fowl into 8 pieces. 将这只家禽切成8块。
  - 补充
