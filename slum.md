- slum
  - 发音
    - 英 `/slʌm/`
    - 美 `/slʌm/`
  - 词义
    - n. 贫民窟
    - `a house or an area of a city that is in very bad condition, where very poor people live`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `slummy` 贫民区的；贫民窟的
  - 近义词
    - n. [经]贫民窟；陋巷；脏乱的地方
      - `bustee`
      - `rookery`
  - 例句
    - `a slum area`
      - 贫民区
    - `slum housing`
      - 贫民区的住房
    - `the slums of London`
      - 伦敦的贫民区
