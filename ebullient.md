- ebullient
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/i'bʌljənt/`
    - 美
      - `/ɪ'bʌlɪənt/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 沸腾的；热情洋溢的
        - `If you describe someone as ebullient, you mean that they are lively and full of enthusiasm or excitement about something`
  - 记忆
    - e + bull (公牛) + ient → 像公牛一样的 → 热情洋溢的
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `ebullience`
        - 奔放；兴高采烈；沸腾；冒泡
      - `ebullition`
        - [物] 沸腾；感情迸发；冒泡
  - 近义词
    - adj.
      - 热情洋溢的；沸腾的
        - `boiling`
        - `young-eyed`
  - 例句
    - `...the ebullient Russian president.`
      - …精力充沛的俄罗斯总统。
