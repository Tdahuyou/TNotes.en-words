- sacrilegious
  - 发音
    - 英 /,sækrɪ'lɪdʒəs/
    - 美 /,sækrɪ'lɪdʒəs/
  - 词义
    - adj. 该受天谴的，亵渎神明的
      - If someone's behaviour or actions are sacrilegious, they show great disrespect toward something holy or toward something that people think should be respected
  - 同根词
    - adv. sacrilegiously 冒渎地
    - n. sacrilege 冒渎；亵渎圣物；悖理逆天的行为
    - n. sacrilegiousness 冒渎
  - 近义词
    - adj. 该受天谴的，亵渎神明的
      - blasphemous
  - 例句
    - A number of churches were looted and sacrilegious acts committed. 许多教堂被洗劫了，还发生了亵渎行为。
