- verdict
  - 发音
    - 英 /'vɜːrdɪkt/
    - 美 /'vɝdɪkt/
  - 词义
    - n. 裁定；判断；意见
      - an official decision made in a court of law, especially about whether someone is guilty of a crime or how a death happened
  - 记忆
    - ver(真实的) + dict(说) → 说出事实 → 裁定
  - 近义词
    - n. 结论；裁定
      - conclusion
      - ruling
  - 例句
    - The verdict was ‘not guilty’. 裁决为“无罪”。
  - 补充
