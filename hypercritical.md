- hypercritical
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ˌhaipə'kritikəl/`
    - 美
      - `/'haɪpɚ'krɪtɪkl/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 吹毛求疵的，过分苛刻的
        - `too eager to criticize other people and things, especially about small details`
  - 记忆
    - hyper (过分地) + crit (判断) + ical (…的) → 过分地讲求判断 → 吹毛求疵的
  - 近义词
    - adj.
      - 吹毛求疵的，苛评的
        - `supercritical`
        - `captiously`
