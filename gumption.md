- gumption
  - 发音
    - 英 /'gʌmpʃn/
    - 美 /'ɡʌmpʃən/
  - 词义
    - n. 进取心，魄力；精明强干
      - the ability and determination to decide what needs to be done and to do it
  - 同根词
    - adj. gumptious 机灵的；精明能干的；精力充沛的
  - 近义词
    - n. 进取心；气概；精力
      - enterprise
      - energy
      - steam
      - push
      - vigor
  - 例句
    - At least she had the gumption to phone me. 至少她有这个头脑，给我打了电话。
