- burlesque
  - 发音
    - 英 `/bɜːr'lesk/`
    - 美 `/bɝ'lɛsk/`
  - 词义
    - n. 讽刺或滑稽的戏剧，滑稽剧
    - `speech, acting, or writing in which a serious subject is made to seem silly or an unimportant subject is treated in a serious way`
  - 记忆
    - “不如乐死去” → 玩笑话 → 滑稽剧
  - 近义词
    - n. 作戏；滑稽戏
      - `antimasque`
    - adj. 滑稽的；可笑的
      - `funny`
      - `ridiculous`
  - 例句
    - `The book read like a black comic burlesque.`
      - 这本书读起来就像一部黑色滑稽剧。
