- subsist
  - 发音
    - 英 /səb'sɪst/
    - 美 /səb'sɪst/
  - 词义
    - vi. 存活，生存
      - to stay alive when you only have small amounts of food or money
  - 记忆
    - sub(在…下面) + sist(站立) → 站在下面 → 生存
  - 同根词
    - n. subsistence 生活；生存；存在
  - 近义词
    - vi. 存在；维持生活
      - exist
      - occur
    - vt. 供养
      - maintain
      - support
      - sustain
      - provide for
  - 短语
    - subsist on 靠…生存
  - 例句
    - The prisoners subsisted on one mug of the worst quality porridge three times a day. 犯人一天三次靠一杯最差的粥维持生活。
