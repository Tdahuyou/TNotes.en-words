- incite
  - 发音
    - 英 `/in'sait/`
    - 美 `/ɪn'saɪt/`
  - 词义
    - v. 激动，煽动
    - `to deliberately encourage people to fight, argue etc`
  - 记忆
    - in + cite (召唤) → 召唤人们 → 煽动
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `incitement` 煽动，刺激；激励物
      - `incitation` 激励；刺激；煽动；诱因
  - 近义词
    - vt. 煽动；激励；刺激
      - `fan`
      - `stimulate`
      - `egg`
      - `power`
  - 例句
    - `They were charged with inciting racial hatred.`
      - 他们被指控煽动种族仇恨。
