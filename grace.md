- grace
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/greɪs/`
    - 美
      - `/ɡres/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 优美,雅致
        - `a smooth way of moving that looks natural, relaxed, and attractive`
    - v.
      - 使优雅
        - `to make a place or an object look more attractive`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `graceful`
        - 优雅的；优美的
    - adv.
      - `gracefully`
        - 优雅地；温文地
      - `gracelessly`
        - 笨拙地；不优美地
    - n.
      - `gracefulness`
        - 温文，优雅；优美
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 优雅；恩惠；魅力；慈悲
        - `charm`
        - `politesse`
  - 例句
    - `Lena moved with the grace of a dancer.`
      - 莉娜走路姿势有着舞者般的优美。
