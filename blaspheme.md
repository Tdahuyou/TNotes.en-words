- blaspheme
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/blæs'fiːm/`
    - 美
      - `/blæs'fim/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 亵渎；咒骂，辱骂
        - `to speak in a way that insults God or people’s religious beliefs, or to use the names of God and holy things when swearing`
  - 记忆
    - bla (伤害) + s + phe (说话) + me → 说了些伤害我的话 → 辱骂
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `blasphemous`
        - 亵渎神明的；不敬神的
    - adv.
      - `blasphemously`
        - 诽谤地；亵渎地
    - n.
      - `blasphemy`
        - 亵渎神明；轻视上帝或神祗
      - `blasphemer`
        - 亵渎者
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 亵渎；咒骂，辱骂
        - `abuse`
        - `dishallow`
  - 例句
    - `"Don't blaspheme," my mother said.`
      - “不要亵渎神明，”我妈妈说。
