- vivacity
  - 发音
    - 英 /vi'væsəti/
    - 美 /vi'væsəti/
  - 词义
    - n. 活泼
      - If you say that someone has vivacity, you mean that they are lively, exciting, and attractive
  - 记忆
    - viv (生命) + acity → 活泼
  - 同根词
    - adj. vivacious 活泼的；快活的；有生气的
    - adj. vivace 活泼的
    - adv. vivace 活泼地
    - adv. vivaciously 愉快地；活泼地；快活地
    - n. vividness 生动；活泼；明亮
  - 近义词
    - n. 活泼；快活；精神充沛
      - animation
      - sunshine
      - brightness
      - volatility
  - 例句
    - He was charmed by her vivacity and high spirits. 她的活泼与兴高采烈的情绪把他迷住了。
    - We were all affected by the vivacity of this lovely little boy. 这个可爱的小男孩的快活使我们都受到感染。
