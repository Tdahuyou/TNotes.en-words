- embroider
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ɪm'brɒɪdə; em-/`
    - 美
      - `/ɪm'brɔɪdɚ/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 刺绣，修饰
        - `to decorate cloth by sewing a pattern, picture, or words on it with coloured threads`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `embroidery`
        - 刺绣；刺绣品；粉饰
      - `embroiderer`
        - 绣花机；刺绣工
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 刺绣；装饰；镶边
        - `paint`
        - `hang`
  - 例句
    - `a richly embroidered jacket`
      - 刺绣华美的夹克衫
