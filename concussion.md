- concussion
  - 发音
    - 英 /kən'kʌʃ(ə)n/
    - 美 /kən'kʌʃən/
  - 词义
    - n. 激动,冲击,[医]震荡
      - a small amount of damage to the brain that makes you lose consciousness or feel sick for a short time, usually caused by something hitting your head
  - 同根词
    - vt. concuss 震动；恐吓
  - 近义词
    - n. 冲击；[医]震荡；脑震荡
      - brunt
      - impingement
  - 短语
    - cerebral concussion 脑震荡
    - brain concussion n. 脑震荡
  - 例句
    - I had a concussion and a lot of scrapes and bruises. 我脑部遭受了震荡，还有许多擦伤和瘀伤。
  - 补充
