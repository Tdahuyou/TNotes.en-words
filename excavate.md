- excavate
  - 发音
    - 英 /'ekskəveɪt/
    - 美 /'ɛkskə'vet/
  - 词义
    - vt. 挖掘；掘出
      - if a scientist or archaeologist excavates an area of land, they dig carefully to find ancient objects, bones etc
  - 记忆
    - ex(出) + cav(洞) + ate(动词后缀) → 挖出洞 → 挖掘； 掘出
  - 同根词
    - n. excavation 挖掘，发掘
    - n. excavator 挖掘机；开凿者；打洞机
  - 近义词
    - vt. 挖掘；开凿
      - to dig
      - tap into
  - 例句
    - Schliemann excavated the ancient city of Troy. 谢里曼发掘出特洛伊古城。
