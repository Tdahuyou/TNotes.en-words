- aquiline
  - 发音
    - 英 /'ækwɪlaɪn/
    - 美 /'ækwɪlaɪn/
  - 词义
    - adj. 鹰的，似鹰的
      - If someone has an aquiline nose or profile, their nose is large, thin, and usually curved
  - 记忆
    - aquil(鹰) + ine → 鹰的
  - 近义词
    - adj. 鹰的；似鹰的，像鹰的；钩状的
      - accipitral
      - falconine
  - 例句
    - He had a thin aquiline nose and deep-set brown eyes. 他长着细长的鹰钩鼻和一双深陷的褐色眼睛。
  - 补充
