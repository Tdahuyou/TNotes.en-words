- scissors
  - 发音
    - 英 /'sɪzəz/
    - 美 /'sɪzɚz/
  - 词义
    - n. [复数]剪刀
      - a tool for cutting paper, cloth etc, made of two sharp blades fastened together in the middle, with holes for your finger and thumb
  - 记忆
    - sciss(分开)+ors→能把物品分开的东西→剪刀， 剪子
  - 同根词
    - n. scissor 剪刀
    - vt. scissor 用剪刀剪；删去
  - 近义词
    - n. [五金]剪刀；剪式跳法
      - forfex
      - cutting tools
  - 例句
    - a pair of scissors 一把剪刀
  - 补充
