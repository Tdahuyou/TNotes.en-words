- relocate
  - 发音
    - 英 /riːlə(ʊ)'keɪt/
    - 美 /,ri'loket/
  - 词义
    - v. 重新部署
      - if a person or business relocates, or if they are relocated, they move to a different place
  - 同根词
    - n. relocation 重新安置；再布置，变换布置
  - 近义词
    - vt. 重新安置；迁移
      - remove
      - to migrate
  - 例句
    - If the company was to relocate, most employees would move. 如果公司要迁移，大多数员工得搬家。
  - 补充
