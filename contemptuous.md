- contemptuous
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/kən'temptʃuəs/`
    - 美
      - `/kən'tɛmptʃuəs/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 轻蔑的，侮辱的
        - `showing that you think someone or something deserves no respect`
  - 记忆
    - 来自contempt (n. 轻视)
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `contemptuously`
        - 轻蔑地
  - 近义词
    - adj.
      - 轻蔑的；侮辱的
        - `scornful`
        - `sniffish`
  - 例句
    - `a contemptuous glance`
      - 轻蔑的一瞥
