- served
  - 发音
    - 英 /sɜːvd/
    - 美 /sɜːrvd/
  - 词义
    - v. 服务；供应；服刑（serve 的过去式和过去分词）
      - Performed duties for others; provided food/drink; completed a sentence.
  - 同根词
    - v. serve 服务；发球
    - n. service 服务；服役
    - n. server 服务器；侍者
    - n. servant 仆人；公仆
    - adj. serving 用于盛食物的
  - 近义词
    - v. 服务
      - assisted
      - attended
      - aided
      - worked
    - v. 供应
      - provided
      - furnished
      - delivered
      - supplied
  - 短语
    - served as 担任
    - served with 随...提供；送达法律文件
    - served time 服刑
    - served up 端上食物
    - served notice 正式通知
  - 例句
    - The waiter served the main course with elegant presentation. 服务员以优雅的摆盘方式上了主菜。
    - She served as ambassador to France for five years. 她担任驻法大使五年。
    - He served two years in prison for the offense. 他因该罪行服刑两年。
    - Tennis players must keep both feet behind the baseline when serving. 网球运动员发球时必须双脚站在底线后。
    - The court papers were served to the defendant yesterday. 法院文件已于昨日送达被告。
  - 补充
    - 核心含义是履行责任：餐饮业指上菜服务（served food），法律指送达文件（served papers），体育指发球动作（tennis serve）。在军事/公务场景表示任职期（served as officer），司法系统指服刑（served sentence）。含被动意味时强调已完成状态。
