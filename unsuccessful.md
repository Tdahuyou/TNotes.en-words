- unsuccessful
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ʌnsək'sesfʊl; -f(ə)l/`
    - 美
      - `/,ʌnsək'sɛsfl/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 不成功的,失败的
        - `not having a successful result or not achieving what you wanted to achieve`
  - 同根词
    - adv.
      - `unsuccessfully`
        - 失败地；无用
  - 近义词
    - adj.
      - 不成功的；失败的
        - `failed`
        - `losing`
  - 例句
    - `an unsuccessful attempt to climb Qomolangma`
      - 攀登珠穆朗玛峰的失败尝试
