- township
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'taʊnʃɪp/`
    - 美 `/'taʊnʃɪp/`
  - 词义
    - n. 镇区；小镇
    - `a town in Canada or the US that has some local government`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `town` 城镇，市镇；市内商业区
  - 近义词
    - n. 镇区；小镇
      - `little town`
  - 短语
    - `township enterprises` 乡镇企业 
    - `township and village enterprises` 乡镇企业 
  - 例句
    - `...her 20 years of service with the township and county.`
      - ...她为镇区和郡服务的20年。
