- nonplus
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ˌnɔn'plʌs/`
    - 美 `/,nɔn'plʌs, 'nɔnplʌs/`
  - 词义
    - v. 使困惑
    - `to put at a loss; confound `
  - 记忆
    - non (不) + plus (增加； 有利的) → 不利 → 窘困
  - 近义词
    - n. 迷惑；困惑
      - `confusion`
      - `delusion`
    - vt. 使困惑
      - `bepuzzle`
      - `perplex`
  - 例句
    - `he was nonplussed by the sudden announcement`
