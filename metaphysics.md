- metaphysics
  - 发音
    - 英 `/metə'fɪzɪks/`
    - 美 `/,mɛtə'fɪzɪks/`
  - 词义
    - n. 形而上学,玄学,纯粹哲学,宇宙哲学
    - `the part of  philosophy  that is concerned with trying to understand and describe the nature of truth, life, and  reality `
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `metaphysical` 形而上学的；超自然的；玄学派诗歌的
  - 近义词
    - n. 玄学，形而上学
      - `ontologies`
