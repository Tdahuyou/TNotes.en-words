- libel
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'laɪbl/`
    - 美 `/'laɪbl/`
  - 词义
    - vt. 诽谤
    - `to write or print a libel against someone`
    - n. 诽谤；诽谤性文字
    - `when someone writes or prints untrue statements about someone so that other people could have a bad opinion of them`
  - 记忆
    - lib(文字) + el → 以文字损害名誉 → (文字)诽谤
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `libellous` 诽谤的；损害名誉的（等于libelous）
  - 近义词
    - n. 诽谤罪；诋毁；毁坏名誉的东西；控诉书
      - `denigration`
      - `traducement`
    - vt. 中伤；控告；对…进行诽谤
      - `law`
      - `traduce`
  - 例句
    - `The newspaper which libelled him had already offered compensation.`
      - 那家诽谤他的报纸已经提出赔偿。
