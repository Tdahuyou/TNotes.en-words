- prostrate
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'prɒstreɪt/`
    - 美 `/'prɑstret/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 降伏的,俯卧的
    - `lying on your front with your face towards the ground`
    - v. 使屈服
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `prostration` 平伏；跪倒；虚脱
  - 近义词
    - adj. 俯卧的；拜倒的；降伏的；沮丧的
      - `blue`
      - `prone`
      - `disappointed`
  - 例句
    - `They found him lying prostrate on the floor.`
      - 他们发现他趴在地板上。
