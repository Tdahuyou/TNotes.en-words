- stately
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'steɪtlɪ/`
    - 美 `/'stetli/`
  - 词义
    - adj. 庄严的,堂皇的
    - `done slowly and with a lot of ceremony`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `stateliness` 威严；庄严
  - 近义词
    - adj. 庄严的；堂皇的，宏伟的
      - `grand`
      - `magnificent`
      - `solemn`
      - `sacred`
      - `majestic`
  - 短语
    - `stately home` n. 开放供参观的豪华古宅 
  - 例句
    - `the stately progress of the procession`
      - 游行队伍缓慢而庄严的行进
