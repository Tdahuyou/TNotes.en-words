- respectively
  - 发音
    - 英 /rɪ'spektɪvlɪ/
    - 美 /rɪ'spɛktɪvli/
  - 词义
    - adv. 分别地,各个地
      - in the same order as the things you have just mentioned
  - 同根词
    - adj. respective 分别的，各自的
  - 近义词
    - adv. 分别地；各自地，独自地
      - separately
      - alone
      - in several
  - 例句
    - The cups and saucers cost £5 and £3 respectively. 茶杯和茶碟的价钱分别为5英镑和3英镑。
  - 补充
