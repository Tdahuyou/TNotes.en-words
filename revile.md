- revile
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ri'vail/`
    - 美 `/rɪ'vaɪl/`
  - 词义
    - v. 辱骂，斥责
    - `to express hatred of someone or something`
  - 记忆
    - re + vile (卑鄙的， 邪恶的) → 辱骂
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `revilement` 辱骂；诽谤
  - 近义词
    - vt. 辱骂；斥责
      - `abuse`
      - `tongue`
  - 例句
    - `The President was now reviled by the same party he had helped to lead.`
      - 如今谩骂总统的正是他曾经协助领导的那个政党。
