- subpoena
  - 发音
    - 英 `/sə'piːnə/`
    - 美 `/sə'pinə/`
  - 词义
    - v. 传讯
    - `to order someone to come to a court of law and be a  witness `
    - n. 【律】传票
    - `a written order to come to a court of law and be a  witness `
  - 记忆
    - sub(下面) + poena(＝penalty惩罚) → 接下来可能受到惩罚 → 传讯
  - 近义词
    - vt. 传唤，传审；传讯
      - `cite`
      - `arraign`
    - n. [法]传票
      - `citation`
      - `summons`
  - 例句
    - `James was subpoenaed as a witness.`
      - 詹姆斯被传唤出庭作证。
