- dispensation
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ˌdispen'seiʃən/`
    - 美 `/,dispen'seiʃən/`
  - 词义
    - n. 分配；豁免
    - `A dispensation is special permission to do something that is normally not allowed`
  - 记忆
    - 来自dispense (v. 分配， 分发)
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `dispenser` 药剂师；施与者；分配者；自动售货机
      - `dispensability` 可分配；可特别恕免；可宽恕
    - vi.
      - `dispense` 免除，豁免
    - vt.
      - `dispense` 分配，分发；免除；执行
  - 近义词
    - n. 分配；免除；豁免；天命
      - `allocation`
      - `distribution`
      - `assignment`
      - `destiny`
      - `immunity`
  - 例句
    - `They were promised dispensation from military service.`
      - 他们被许诺将被特免不用服兵役。
