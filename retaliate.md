- retaliate
  - 发音
    - 英 /ri'tæliˌeit/
    - 美 /rɪ'tælɪ'et/
  - 词义
    - v. 报复
      - to do something bad to someone because they have done something bad to you
  - 记忆
    - re (回) + tali (邪恶) + ate → 以恶制恶， 以牙还牙 → 报复
  - 同根词
    - adj. retaliatory 报复的
    - n. retaliation 报复；反击；回敬
  - 近义词
    - vi. 报复；回敬
      - get back on
      - get even with
    - vt. 报复
      - get back on
      - get even with
      - get back at
  - 例句
    - I was sorely tempted to retaliate. 我非常想报复。
