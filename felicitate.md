- felicitate
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/fə'lɪsɪteɪt/`
    - 美
      - `/undefined/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 祝贺，庆祝
        - `to wish joy to; congratulate `
  - 记忆
    - felic(幸福的) + itate → 使…幸福 → 祝贺
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `felicitation`
        - 祝贺；庆祝；庆贺
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 庆贺；庆祝
        - `commemorate`
        - `congratulate`
  - 例句
    - `Japan should felicitate the opening of branches of Japanese vessel building industries along the Indian docks and port.`
      - 日本该庆祝印度港口里日本造船分厂的开设。
