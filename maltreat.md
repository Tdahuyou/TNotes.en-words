- maltreat
  - 发音
    - 英 /mæl'triːt/

  - 词义
    - v. 虐待；滥用；粗暴对待
      - to treat a person or animal cruelly
  - 同根词
    - n. maltreatment 虐待；粗暴对待
  - 近义词
    - vt. 虐待；滥用；粗暴对待
      - abuse
      - rough
      - strain
  - 例句
    - The hostages said they were hungry but had not been maltreated. 人质说他们挨了饿，但没有受到虐待。
