- suffuse
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/sə'fjuːz/`
    - 美
      - `/sə'fjuz/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 充满
        - `if warmth, colour, liquid etc suffuses something or someone, it covers or spreads through them`
  - 记忆
    - suf (下) + fus (流动) + e → 满得向下流 → 充满
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `suffusion`
        - 充满；弥漫；遍布
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 充满；弥漫
        - `abound with`
        - `be imbued with`
  - 例句
    - `The light of the setting sun suffused the clouds.`
      - 落日的霞光映红了云层。
    - `Hot colour suffused her cheeks.`
      - 她的脸涨得又红又烫。
