- presuppose
  - 发音
    - 英 /priːsə'pəʊz/
    - 美 /,prisə'poz/
  - 词义
    - v. 假定；预料；以…为先决条件
      - to depend on something that is believed to exist or to be true
  - 同根词
    - n. presupposition 假定；预想；臆测
  - 近义词
    - vt. 假定；预料；以…为先决条件
      - expect
      - to suppose
  - 例句
    - The idea of heaven presupposes the existence of God. 天堂的说法首先认定了上帝的存在。
