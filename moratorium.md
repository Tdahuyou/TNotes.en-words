- moratorium
  - 发音
    - 英 /ˌmɔrə'tɔːriəm/
    - 美 /,mɔrə'tɔrɪəm/
  - 词义
    - n. 延期偿付
      - a law or an agreement that gives people more time to pay their debts
  - 记忆
    - morat (延误) + orium → 延期偿付
  - 近义词
    - n. 暂停，中止；[金融]延期偿付
      - hang
      - temporary suspension
  - 例句
    - a two-year moratorium on interest payments 两年延后付息
  - 补充
