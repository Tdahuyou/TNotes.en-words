- emollient
  - 发音
    - 英 /i'mɔlɪənt/
    - 美 /ɪ'mɑlɪənt/
  - 词义
    - adj. 柔软的；有缓和作用的
    - n. 润肤剂
      - An emollient is a liquid or cream that you put on your skin to make it softer or to reduce pain
  - 记忆
    - e + moll ( = soft， 软) + ient → 使肌肤变柔软的东西 → 润肤剂
  - 近义词
    - n. [药]润肤剂；软化剂
      - softener
      - softening agent
  - 例句
    - Almond oil is renowned for its soothing, emollient properties. 杏仁油以其舒缓和滋润功效闻名。
