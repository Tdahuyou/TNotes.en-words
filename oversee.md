- oversee
  - 发音
    - 英 `/əʊvə'siː/`
    - 美 `/'ovɚ'si/`
  - 词义
    - v. 监督，监管
    - `to be in charge of a group of workers and check that a piece of work is done satisfactorily`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `overseer` 监督；工头
  - 近义词
    - vt. 监督；审查；俯瞰；偷看到，无意中看到
      - `police`
      - `survey`
  - 例句
    - `A team leader was appointed to oversee the project.`
      - 任命了一位组长来监管该工程。
