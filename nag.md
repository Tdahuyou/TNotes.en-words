- nag
  - 发音
    - 英 /næg/
    - 美 /næɡ/
  - 词义
    - n. 唠叨；老马；竞赛马
      - a person who keeps complaining or asking someone to do something, in an annoying way
    - v. 使烦恼；不断地唠叨
      - to keep asking someone to do something, or to keep complaining to someone about their behaviour, in an annoying way
  - 同根词
    - adj. nagging 唠叨的；挑剔的；使人不得安宁的
    - n. nagging 唠叨；挑剔
    - n. nagger 爱唠叨的人；尽找岔的人
    - v. nagging 唠叨（nag的ing形式）
  - 近义词
    - n. 唠叨；老马；竞赛马
      - jade
      - nattering
    - vt. 使烦恼；不断地唠叨
      - trouble
      - irk
  - 短语
    - nag at v. 挑剔；唠叨；以唠叨来恼人
  - 例句
    - Don’t be such a nag! 别总是唠唠叨叨的！
