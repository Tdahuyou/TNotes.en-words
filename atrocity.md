- atrocity
  - 发音
    - 英 `/ə'trɔsiti/`
    - 美 `/ə'trɑsəti/`
  - 词义
    - n. 残暴；暴行；凶恶
    - `an extremely cruel and violent action, especially during a war`
  - 记忆
    - atroc (凶残) + ity → 凶恶； 残暴
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `atrocious` 凶恶的，残暴的
  - 近义词
    - n. 暴行；凶恶，残暴
      - `enormity`
      - `thuggism`
  - 例句
    - `The killing was cold-blooded, and those who committed this atrocity should be tried and punished.`
      - 这场杀戮非常残酷，那些犯下此暴行的人应该受到审判和惩罚。
