- exempt
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ɪg'zempt/`
    - 美
      - `/ɪg'zɛmpt/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 被免除的，被豁免的
        - `not affected by something, or not having to do it or pay it`
    - vt.
      - 免除，豁免
        - `to give someone permission not to do or pay something`
  - 记忆
    - ex(出) + empt(拿) → 拿出去 → 免除， 豁免
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `exemption`
        - 免除，豁免；免税
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 免除；豁免
        - `discharge`
        - `forgive`
  - 短语
    - `exempt from`
      - 豁免，免除 
    - `exempt from tax`
      - 免税 
    - `tax exempt`
      - 免税的 
  - 例句
    - `Men in college were exempt from military service.`
      - 在校男大学生免服兵役。
