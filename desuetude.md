- desuetude
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/dɪ'sjuːɪtjuːd/`
    - 美
      - `/'dɛswɪ,tjʊd/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 废止，不用
        - `the condition of not being in use or practice; disuse `
  - 记忆
    - de + suet(=suit适合) + ude → 不再适合 → 废止
  - 近义词
    - n.
      - 废止；不用
        - `avoidance`
        - `defeasance`
  - 例句
    - `those ceremonies had fallen into desuetude`
