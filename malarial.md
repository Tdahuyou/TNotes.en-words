- malarial
  - 发音
    - 英 /mə'leriəl/
    - 美 /mə'lɛrɪəl/
  - 词义
    - adj. 患疟疾的；毒气的
      - You can use malarial to refer to things connected with malaria or areas which are affected by malaria
  - 记忆
    - 来自malaria(n. 疟疾)
  - 同根词
    - n. malaria [内科] 疟疾；瘴气
  - 近义词
    - adj. 患疟疾的；毒气的
      - agued
      - miasmatic
  - 例句
    - ...malarial parasites. …疟疾寄生虫。
  - 补充
