- asphyxiate
  - 发音
    - 英 `/əs'fɪksɪeɪt/`
    - 美 `/əs'fɪksɪet/`
  - 词义
    - v. 窒息
    - `to prevent someone from breathing normally, usually so that they die, or stop breathing`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `asphyxiation` 窒息
      - `asphyxia` [医] 窒息
      - `asphyxiator` 动物窒息器；碳酸气灭火器
  - 近义词
    - vt. 使…窒息
      - `strangle`
    - vi. 窒息
      - `choke`
      - `strangulate`
  - 例句
    - `Three people were asphyxiated in the crush for last week's train.`
      - 三个人为了赶上周的火车窒息在人群中。
