- embellishment
  - 发音
    - 英 /ɪm'belɪʃmənt/
    - 美 /ɪm'bɛlɪʃmənt/
  - 词义
    - n. 装饰；装饰品
      - An embellishment is a decoration added to something to make it seem more attractive or interesting
  - 同根词
    - vi. embellish 装饰起来；加以润色
    - vt. embellish 修饰；装饰；润色
  - 近义词
    - n. [服装]装饰，[建]修饰；润色
      - decoration
      - adornment
  - 例句
    - ...Renaissance embellishments. ...文艺复兴时期的装饰。
    - ...public buildings with little bits of decoration and embellishment. ...鲜有装饰的公共建筑物。
