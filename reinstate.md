- reinstate
  - 发音
    - 英 `/riːɪn'steɪt/`
    - 美 `/,riɪn'stet/`
  - 词义
    - v. 使恢复；使复原
    - `if someone is reinstated, they are officially given back their job after it was taken away`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `reinstatement` 恢复；复原；复职
  - 近义词
    - vt. 使恢复；使复原
      - `bring around`
  - 例句
    - `California reinstated the death penalty in 1977.`
      - 加利福尼亚于1977年恢复了死刑。
