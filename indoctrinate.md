- indoctrinate
  - 发音
    - 英 /ɪn'dɒktrɪneɪt/
    - 美 /ɪn'dɑktrɪnet/
  - 词义
    - v. 灌输；教导
      - to train someone to accept a particular set of beliefs, especially political or religious ones, and not consider any others
  - 同根词
    - n. indoctrination 教化；教导
  - 近义词
    - vt. 灌输；教导
      - wise
      - imbue
  - 例句
    - People were indoctrinated not to question their leaders. 人们被灌输对领导人不得置疑的观念。
  - 补充
