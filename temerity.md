- temerity
  - 发音
    - 英 /ti'merəti/
    - 美 /tə'mɛrəti/
  - 词义
    - n. 鲁莽；冒失
      - when someone says or does something in a way that shows a lack of respect for other people and is likely to offend them
  - 记忆
    - 来自temer (轻率) + ity → 轻率 → 鲁莽
  - 同根词
    - adj. temerarious 鲁莽的；不顾一切的；极大胆的
  - 近义词
    - n. 鲁莽，冒失；蛮勇
      - liberty
      - precipitation
  - 例句
    - He actually had the temerity to tell her to lose weight. 他竟然放肆地叫她减肥。
  - 补充
