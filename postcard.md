- postcard
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'pəʊs(t)kɑːd/`
    - 美 `/'post'kɑrd/`
  - 词义
    - n. 明信片
    - `a card that can be sent in the post without an envelope, especially one with a picture on it`
  - 记忆
    - post(邮递)+card(卡片)→邮递的卡片→明信片
  - 近义词
    - n. 明信片
      - `postal card`
  - 短语
    - `picture postcard` 美术明信片 
  - 例句
    - `Don’t forget to send us a postcard!`
      - 别忘了给我们寄张明信片！
