- thrall
  - 发音
    - 英 /θrɔːl/
    - 美 /θrɔl/
  - 词义
    - n. 奴隶；束缚；奴役
      - If you say that someone is in thrall to a person or thing, you mean that they are completely in their power or are greatly influenced by them
  - 记忆
    - thr (看作throw， 扔) + all (一切) → 抛弃一切束缚
  - 同根词
    - n. thralldom 束缚；奴役；奴隶的身分
  - 近义词
    - n. 奴隶；束缚
      - chain
      - restriction
      - slave
  - 例句
    - He is not in thrall to the media. 他不受制于媒体。
    - Tomorrow's children will be even more in the thrall of the silicon chip. 以后的孩子们对电脑芯片的依赖甚至会更深。
  - 补充
