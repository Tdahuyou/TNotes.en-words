- coddle
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'kɔdl/`
    - 美 `/'kɔdl/`
  - 词义
    - v. 娇养，溺爱；悉心照料
    - `to treat someone in a way that is too kind and gentle and that protects them from pain or difficulty`
  - 记忆
    - 放入摇篮 (cradle) 悉心照料 (coddle)
  - 近义词
    - vt. 娇养；溺爱（等于mollycoddle）；用文火煮
      - `cocker`
      - `dote upon`
  - 例句
    - `Don’t coddle the child – he’s fine!`
      - 别太娇惯那孩子——他没事的！
