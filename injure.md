- injure
  - 发音
    - 英 /'ɪndʒə/
    - 美 /'ɪndʒɚ/
  - 词义
    - v. 伤害，损害
      - to say unfair or unpleasant things that hurt someone’s pride, feelings etc
  - 同根词
    - adj. injured 受伤的；受损害的
    - adj. injurious 有害的；诽谤的
    - adv. injuriously 有害地；伤害地
    - n. injury 伤害，损害；受伤处
  - 近义词
    - vt. 伤害，损害
      - hurt
      - knife
  - 例句
    - A number of bombs have exploded, seriously injuring at least five people. 一些炸弹爆炸了，至少5人严重受伤。
  - 补充
