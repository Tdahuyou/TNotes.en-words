- dangle
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'dæŋg(ə)l/`
    - 美
      - `/'dæŋɡl/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 悬挂，摇摆，使晃动
        - `If something dangles from somewhere or if you dangle it somewhere, it hangs or swings loosely`
  - 记忆
    - “荡够” → 悬荡， 悬摆
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `dangling`
        - 悬挂的；摇摆的
    - v.
      - `dangling`
        - 摇晃（dangle的ing形式）
  - 例句
    - `A gold bracelet dangled from his left wrist.`
      - 一只金手镯在他左腕上晃来晃去。
