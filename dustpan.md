- dustpan
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'dʌs(t)pæn/`
    - 美 `/'dʌstpæn/`
  - 词义
    - n. 簸箕
    - `a flat container with a handle that you use with a brush to remove dust and waste from the floor`
  - 近义词
    - n. 簸箕
      - `garbage container`
  - 例句
    - `Have you got a dustpan and brush?`
      - 你有畚箕和刷子吗？
