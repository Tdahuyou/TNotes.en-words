- contrary
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'kɑːntreri/`
    - 美
      - `/'kɑntrɛri/`
  - 词义
    - adj.
      - 相反的，逆的
        - `contrary ideas, opinions, or actions are completely different and opposed to each other`
    - n.
      - 相反，反面
        - `used to add to a negative statement, to disagree with a negative statement by someone else, or to answer no to a question`
  - 记忆
    - contra(反) + ry → 相反的， 逆的
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `contrarious`
        - 对抗的；作对的
    - adv.
      - `contrarily`
        - 相反地；反之；反对地
      - `contrariwise`
        - 反之，相反；顽固地
      - `contrariously`
        - 相反地；不利地
    - n.
      - `contrarian`
        - 做法与一般大众想法相反的投资人（如买冷门股票）；（与其他人）持相反意见者
      - `contrariety`
        - 相反物；反对；矛盾
      - `contrariness`
        - 反对；矛盾；乖张
      - `contrariousness`
        - 不利；相反
  - 近义词
    - adj.
      - 相反的；对立的
        - `opposite`
        - `adverse`
        - `counter`
        - `against`
        - `cross`
    - adv.
      - 相反地
        - `counter`
        - `backward`
        - `conversely`
        - `inversely`
    - n.
      - 相反；反面
        - `inverse`
        - `verso`
  - 短语
    - `on the contrary`
      - 正相反 
    - `to the contrary`
      - 相反 
    - `quite the contrary`
      - 恰恰相反 ，正相反 
    - `to be contrary to`
      - 违背；与…相反 
  - 例句
    - `Two contrary views emerged.`
      - 出现了两种相反的观点。
    - `The men shouted contrary orders.`
      - 这些人大声喊叫的命令互相矛盾。
