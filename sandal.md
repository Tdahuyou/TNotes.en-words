- sandal
  - 发音
    - 英 `/'sænd(ə)l/`
    - 美 `/'sændl/`
  - 词义
    - n. 凉鞋,檀香
    - `a light shoe that is fastened onto your foot by bands of leather or cloth, and is worn in warm weather`
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `sandalwood` 檀香，白檀；檀香木
  - 近义词
    - n. [服装]凉鞋；檀香；檀香木；便鞋
      - `santal`
      - `almug`
  - 例句
    - `a pair of sandals`
      - 一双凉鞋
