- reimburse
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/ˌriːim'bəːs/`
    - 美
      - `/,riɪm'bɝs/`
  - 词义
    - v.
      - 偿还
        - `to pay money back to someone when their money has been spent or lost`
  - 记忆
    - re + im (进入) + burse (钱包) → 借出的钱重新进入钱包 → 偿还
  - 同根词
    - n.
      - `reimbursement`
        - 退还，偿还；赔偿
  - 近义词
    - vt.
      - 偿还；赔偿
        - `replace`
        - `tender`
  - 例句
    - `I'll be happy to reimburse you for any expenses you've had.`
      - 我很乐意报销你的所有花费。
