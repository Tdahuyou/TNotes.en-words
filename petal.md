- petal
  - 发音
    - 英
      - `/'pet(ə)l/`
    - 美
      - `/'pɛtl/`
  - 词义
    - n.
      - 花瓣
        - `one of the coloured parts of a flower that are shaped like leaves`
  - 同根词
    - adj.
      - `petalless`
        - 无瓣的
      - `petaloid`
        - 似花瓣的
  - 例句
    - `rose petals`
      - 玫瑰花瓣
    - `The flower has seven petals.`
      - 这种花有七片花瓣。
